EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Highway Authority Malaysia (Incorporation)
Act 1980 (“Act 231”).

2. Clause 1 contains the short title and the provision on the commencement
of the proposed Act.

3. Clause 2 seeks to amend the long title of Act 231 to exclude inter-urban
highway from the scope of Act 231.

4. Clause 3 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 231 to introduce new definitions
and to amend certain existing definitions.

5. Clause 4 seeks to amend paragraph 4(1)(f) of Act 231 as a consequence
to the new definition of “Director General” introduced into section 2
of Act 231.

6. Clause 5 seeks to introduce a new section 7a into Act 231 to require
the disclosure of any interest by the members of the Authority and its committee.

7. Clause 6 seeks to amend section 9 of Act 231 to provide that the Authority
may appoint temporarily any officer of the Authority to act as the Director General
in the absence of the Director General.

8. Clause 7 seeks to amend section 11 of Act 231.

_Subclause 7(a)_ seeks to introduce new functions of the Authority,

among others, to advise the Federal Government on any matters relating
to highways, to formulate standard for highways and to undertake the planning,
design, construction, operation, management and maintenance of highways.

_Subclause 7(b)_ seeks to empower the Authority to make regulations,

with the approval of the Minister, on certain matters in addition to the existing
matters specified in subsection 11(2) of Act 231.

9. Clause 8 seeks to amend section 12 of Act 231 to provide for the payment
of any excess of moneys in the Fund under paragraph 12(2)(a) of Act 231
in relation to the performance of functions of the Authority into the Federal
Consolidated Fund.


-----

12 _Bill_

10. _Clause 9 seeks to amend section 16 of Act 231 to provide that_

the Authority may borrow any money required by the Authority subject
to the approval of the Minister and the Minister of Finance.

11. _Clause 10 seeks to introduce a new section 17a into Act 231. The proposed_

section 17a deals with the power of the Authority to establish companies with
the approval of the Minister and the Minister of Finance.

12. _Clause 11 seeks to amend section 25 of Act 231 to include the penalty under_

section 25 of Act 231 into the general penalty under section 26 of Act 231.

13. _Clause 12_ seeks to amend section 26 of Act 231 to increase the fine

for general penalty from five thousand ringgit to twenty thousand ringgit.

14. _Clause 13_ seeks to introduce new sections 26a and 26b into Act 231.

The proposed section 26a seeks to provide that no prosecution shall be

instituted for any offence under Act 231 or any regulations made under
Act 231 except by or with the written consent of the Public Prosecutor.

The proposed section 26b seeks to empower the Minister, with the approval

of the Public Prosecutor, to make regulations prescribing any offence
under Act 231 or any regulations made under Act 231 to be compoundable
as well as the method and procedure for compounding such offence.
This _clause also seeks to empower any officer authorized by the Authority_
to implement the compounding of such offences with the consent in writing
of the Public Prosecutor.

15. _Clause 14 seeks to substitute section 27 of Act 231 to deal with_
the obligation of secrecy in relation to any disclosure of information
or document obtained under Act 231.

16. Clause 15 seeks to introduce new sections 33, 34 and 35 into Act 231.

The proposed section 33 seeks to provide for the protection of the Authority,

any member of the Authority, member of a committee, officer or servant
of the Authority, or agent of the Authority against suits and legal proceedings
under Act 231.

The proposed section 34 seeks to provide for the application

of the Public Authorities Protection Act 1948 [Act 198] to the Authority,
member of the Authority, member of the committee, officer or servant of the
Authority, or agent of the Authority under Act 231.

The proposed section 35 seeks to empower the Minister to amend

the Schedule to Act 231.

17. _Clause 16 seeks to amend the Schedule to Act 231 for the purpose_

of consistency.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial expenditure.
