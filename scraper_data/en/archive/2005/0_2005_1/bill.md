# An Act to amend the Government Investment Act 1983.

 ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:

 Short title

 1. This Act may be cited as the Government Investment (Amendment) Act 2005.

 Amendment of long title

 2. The Government Investment Act 1983 [Act 275], which is referred to as the "principal Act" in this Act, is amended by substituting for the long title the following long title:

 "An Act to provide for the raising of funds by the Government
 of Malaysia in accordance with the Syariah principles and to provide for matters incidental thereto or connected therewith.".

 Amendment of section 1

 3. Section 1 of the principal Act is amended in the short title by substituting for the word "Investment" the word "Funding".

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Government Investment Act 1983 ("Act 275")-

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title of the proposed Act._

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend the long title of Act 275 to reflect the purpose_

of the proposed Act to empower the Government to issue Islamic instruments
under various Syariah principles and for different tenures.


-----

8 _Bill_

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 1 of Act 275 to amend the short title of_

Act 275. The Act is to be known after this amendment as the "Government
Funding Act 1983".

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 275 to delete the definitions of_

"statutory acknowledgement receipt" and "statutory monthly statement" and to
introduce new definitions of "instrument", "notification", "Syariah Advisory
Council" and "return".

6. _Clause 5 seeks to introduce section 2A SO that any instrument issued under_

this Act shall be issued in accordance with Syariah principles as approved by
the Syariah Advisory Council.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend section 3 to enable the Minister on behalf of the_

Government to receive investments in Malaysia and to create and issue instruments
evidencing such investments by any arrangement and by entering into any
transaction with any person as the Minister deems fit.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to amend section 5 to enable the issuance of short, medium_

or long term instruments under the proposed Act.

9. _Clause 8 seeks to introduce section 5A to provide for the Central Bank to_

determine the terms and conditions of instruments or payments for every instrument
issued under the proposed Act.

10. _Clauses 9, 13, 14, 18 and 19 seek to amend Act 275 to reflect the current_

practices of trading and transfer of Islamic instruments issued by the Government
under the scripless system.

11. _Clause 10 seeks to amend section 8 to reflect the payment of returns for_

the Islamic instruments issued by the Government under any concept allowed
by Syariah.

12. _Clauses 11 and 12 seek to amend sections 9 and 9A respectively to provide_

for the payments of the instruments issued under Act 275 to be made in accordance
with the terms and conditions of such instruments.

13. _Clause 20 seeks to provide that any reference to the Government Investment_

Act 1983 in any written law or document shall, after the proposed Act comes
into operation, be construed as references to the "Government Funding Act

1983".

14. Other amendments not specifically dealt with in this Statement are minor

or consequential in nature.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial expenditure.
