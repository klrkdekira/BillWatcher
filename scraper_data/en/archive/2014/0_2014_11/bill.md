_Architects (Amendment)_ 1

A BILL

_i n t i t u l e d_

An Act to amend the Architects Act 1967.

[ ]

**ENACTED** by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:

**Short title and commencement**

**1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Architects (Amendment)**
Act 2014.

(2) Except for paragraphs 3(d) and _(p) and section 23, this_
Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed by the
Minister by notification in the _Gazette and the Minister may_
appoint different dates for the coming into operation of different
provisions of this Act.

(3) Paragraphs 3(d) and (p) and section 23 come into operation
on 1 June 2015.


-----

2 _Bill_

**General amendment**

**2. The Architects Act 1967 [Act 117], which is referred to as**
the “principal Act” in this Act, is amended by substituting for
the words “Professional Architects”, “Professional Architect” and
“a Professional Architect” wherever appearing, except in the definition
of “Professional Architect” in section 3, the words “Architects”,
“Architect” and “an Architect” respectively.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Architects Act 1967 (“Act 117”). The proposed
amendments seek to primarily liberalise the architectural profession in Malaysia
and to provide for related matters.

2. _Clause 1 provides for the short title and the power of the Minister to_
appoint different dates for the commencement of different provisions of the
proposed Act.

3. Clause 2 seeks to provide for the general amendment to Act 117 by
substituting for the words “Professional Architects”, “Professional Architect”
and “a Professional Architect” wherever appearing, except in the definition
of “Professional Architect” in section 3, the words “Architects”, “Architect”
and “an Architect” respectively.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 117 to introduce new definitions_
and to amend existing definitions used in Act 117.

5. Clause 4 seeks to amend section 3 of Act 117 to include an Inspector of
Works and an Architectural Technologist as new architectural professions to
be represented in the membership of the Board.

6. Clause 5 seeks to amend section 4 of Act 117 to enhance the functions
of the Board.

7. Clause 6 seeks to amend section 5 of Act 117 to allow the registration
of foreign architects.


-----

44 _Bill_

8. Clause 7 seeks to amend section 6 of Act 117 to allow the Registrar to
publish in the _Gazette or national newspaper the particulars of an Architect,_
a Graduate Architect, a foreign architect, a Building Draughtsman, an Inspector
of Works, an Architectural Technologist, an Interior Designer, a Graduate
Interior Designer, an architectural consultancy practice rendering architectural
consultancy service, or an interior design consultancy practice rendering interior
design consultancy service whose registration has been suspended, cancelled,
removed and reinstated under Act 117.

9. Clause 8 seeks to amend section 7 of Act 117 to provide restrictions on
unregistered Architects.

10. _Clauses 9 and 10 seek to amend sections 7a and 7b of Act 117 respectively_

to provide for matters relating to architectural consultancy practice and
architectural consultancy services.

11. _Clause 11 seeks to amend section 8 of Act 117 to provide that only an_

Architect or a foreign architect who is residing in Malaysia for not less than
one hundred and eighty days in any one calendar year shall be entitled to
submit plans or drawings to any person or authority in Malaysia.

12. _Clause 13 seeks to amend section 10 of Act 117 to set out the qualifications_

for registration of Architects, _etc._

13. _Clause 14 seeks to amend section 10a of Act 117 to allow registration_

of foreign architects who are consultants to a project, wholly financed by
a foreign government or implemented under any form of arrangement with
the Government of Malaysia.
