EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Poisons Act 1952 (“Act 366”) to provide flexibility
in the handling of poison by allowing the use of document in electronic form
and retail sale of poison by registered pharmacist at or from a premises where
a licensed pharmacist is licensed to retail poisons under Act 366. This Bill also
seeks to enhance certain penalties in Act 366 relating to the use of poisons
and psychotropic substances.

to appoint different dates for the coming into operation of different provisions
of the proposed Act.

and to amend the existing definition of certain terms used in Act 366.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 3 of Act 366 to change the name of_

in line with the name currently used.
