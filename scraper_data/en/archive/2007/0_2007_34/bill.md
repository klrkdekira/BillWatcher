A BILL

_i n t i t u l e d_

An Act to apply a sum from the Consolidated Fund for additional
expenditure for the service of the year 2007 and to appropriate
that sum for certain purposes for that year.

ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:

**Short title**

**1.** This Act may be cited as the Supplementary Supply (2007)
Act 2007.

**Further expenditure for the year 2007**

**2.** The issue is hereby authorized of a sum not exceeding nine
billion seven hundred eighty-six million seven hundred forty-three
thousand five hundred and ten ringgit (RM9,786,743,510) from the
Consolidated Fund for expenditure on the services and purposes
specified in the Schedule for the year 2007 not provided for or
not fully provided for by the Supply Act 2007 [Act A1276].

# Supplementary Supply (2007)

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to provide for the issue from the Consolidated Fund of a sum
of nine billion seven hundred eighty-six million seven hundred forty-three
thousand five hundred and ten ringgit (RM9,786,743,510) for the services
and purposes specified in the Schedule for the year 2007.
