**Short title and commencement**

**1.  (1) This Act may be cited as the Islamic Banking (Amendment)**
Act 2007.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed

by the Minister by notification in the Gazette.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT This Bill seeks to

amend the Islamic Banking Act 1983 ("Act 276").


2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and the provision on the commencement_

of the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 to introduce into Act 276 definitions_

for the terms "international Islamic bank", "foreign institution", "office",
"international Islamic banking business" and "foreign company".

4. _Clause 3 seeks to introduce new Part VA into Act 276 to provide for legal_

framework for the regulation of institutions carrying on international Islamic
banking business.


-----

_Islamic Banking (Amendment)_

The proposed new section 30A provides the general prohibition from carrying

on international Islamic banking business by any person unless the requirements
mentioned therein are satisfied, the breach of which is an offence.

The proposed new section 30B allows an institution incorporated under the

Companies Act 1965 and foreign institution to apply for a licence to carry
on international Islamic banking business subject to certain requirements laid
down in that section.

The proposed new section 30c empowers the Minister, on the recommendation

of the Central Bank, to vary or revoke conditions of a licence.

The proposed new section 30D provides for the minimum capital funds

requirement for international Islamic banks incorporated under the Companies
Act 1965 and minimum net working funds requirement for international Islamic
banks that they need to maintain at all times. The Minister is empowered
to determine the minimum amount on the recommendation of the Central
Bank.

The proposed new section 30E provides that Islamic banks shall be subject

to the provisions of Act 276 in respect of their international Islamic banking
business and the provisions of Act 276 which apply to Islamic banks shall
also apply to international Islamic banks.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 53 of Act 276 to empower the Central_

Bank with the approval of the Minister to make regulations in respect of
any matter relating to the regulation and supervision of international Islamic
banks.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to introduce new section 53A into Act 276 to empower the_

Minister or the Central Bank to issue guidelines, circulars or notes generally
or in respect of any provision of the Act.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend section 56 of Act 276 to empower the Minister_

to exempt any Islamic bank, international Islamic bank or person from all or
any provision of the Act.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill  will not involve the Government in  any extra financial
expenditure.
