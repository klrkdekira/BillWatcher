A BILL

_i n t i t u l e d_

An Act to amend the National Productivity Corporation

(Incorporation) Act 1966.

# [ ]

ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:

**Short title and commencement**

**1. (1)** This Act may be cited as the National Productivity
Corporation (Incorporation) (Amendment) Act 2007.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed by the

Minister by notification in the Gazette.

**Interpretation**

**2.** For the purpose of this Act, "commencement date" means the
date specified in subsection 1(2).

**General amendments**

**3.** The National Productivity Corporation (Incorporation) Act
1966, which in this Act is referred to as the "principal Act", is
amendedâ€”

_(a)_ by substituting for the word "NATIONAL" the word

"MALAYSIA"; and

_(b)_ by substituting for the word "National" wherever it appears

the word "Malaysia".


-----

2 _Bill_

**Construction of reference to the principal Act**

**4.** Where, on and after the commencement date, a reference is
made in any written law to the "National Productivity Corporation
(Incorporation) Act 1966", such reference shall be construed as a
reference to the "Malaysia Productivity Corporation (Incorporation)
Act 1966".

**Change of name of the Corporation**

**5.** (1) The National Productivity Corporation established

under section 3 of the principal Act shall, on and after the
commencement date, be known as the "Malaysia Productivity
Corporation".

(2) AH references to the National Productivity Corporation in any

written law or in any instrument, deed, title, document, bond,
agreement or working arrangement subsisting immediately before the
commencement date, shall, on and after the commencement date, be
construed as reference to the Malaysia Productivity Corporation.

(3) The change of name from "National Productivity Corporation"

to the "Malaysia Productivity Corporation" shall not affect any
powers, rights, privileges, liabilities, duties or obligations of the
National Productivity Corporation, or render defective any legal
proceedings by or against it.

(4) Any legal proceedings that could have been continued or

commenced by or against National Productivity Corporation prior to
the commencement date may be continued or commenced on and
after the commencement date in the name of the Malaysia
Productivity Corporation.

**Liabilities enforceable against the Corporation**

**6.** All existing liabilities incurred by or on behalf of or for
the purposes of National Productivity Corporation may, on and
after the commencement date, be enforced against the Malaysia
Productivity Corporation.

_National Productivity Corporation_

3

_(Incorporation) (Amendment)_

**Vesting of land, property and asset in the Corporation**

7. (1) All lands, property and assets that immediately before the
commencement date were vested in or reserved for the National
Productivity Corporation or any person acting on its behalf
shall, on the commencement date, vest in or be reserved for the
Malaysia Productivity Corporation or any person acting on its
behalf without any conveyance, assignment or transfer.

(2) All lands, property and assets vested in or reserved for the

Malaysia Productivity Corporation by virtue of subsection (1) shall
be so vested in or be so reserved for the Malaysia Productivity
Corporation for the like title or interest and on the like tenure as the
lands, property and assets were vested or reserved immediately before
the commencement date.

**National Productivity Corporation Fund and loan fund**

**8.** (1) All moneys contained in or due to be paid to the National
Productivity Corporation Fund established under section 10 of the
principal Act shall, on the commencement date, be transferred to
and be deemed to be part of the Malaysia Productivity Corporation
Fund.

(2) Any other property belonging to the National Productivity

Corporation Fund immediately before the commencement date shall
become, on the commencement date, the property of the Malaysia
Productivity Corporation Fund.

(3) All moneys contained in the loan fund and any other fund of

the National Productivity Corporation shall, on the commencement
date, be transferred to and deemed to be part of the loan fund or such
other fund of the Malaysia Productivity Corporation.

**Members of the Corporation**

**9.** All members of the National Productivity Corporation shall,
on the commencement date, be deemed to be the members of the
Malaysia Productivity Corporation.

4 _Bill_

**The Committees**

**10.** All Committees and members of the Committees appointed
by the National Productivity Corporation under section 5B of the
principal Act shall, on the commencement date, be deemed to be
the Committees and members thereof appointed by the Malaysia
Productivity Corporation.

**Continuance of service of officers, servants, agents, etc.**

**11. (1)** All officers and servants appointed by the National
Productivity Corporation pursuant to section 5 of the principal
Act immediately before the commencement date shall, on the
commencement date, be deemed to have been appointed by
the Malaysia Productivity Corporation on terms and conditions
of service which are not less favourable than the terms and
conditions of service applicable to them immediately before the
commencement date.

(2) All agents, technical advisors and other persons employed by

the National Productivity Corporation pursuant to section 7A of the
principal Act immediately before the commencement date shall, on
the commencement date, be deemed to have been employed by the
Malaysia Productivity Corporation.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the National Productivity Corporation (Incorporation) Act
1966 ("Act 408") to replace the name National Productivity Corporation with
Malaysia Productivity Corporation.

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and the commencement date of the proposed_

Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to define the term "commencement date" to mean the date of_

coming into operation of the proposed Act.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to make general amendments to Act 408 by replacing the words_

"NATIONAL" and "National" with the words "MALAYSIA" and "Malaysia"
respectively. With this amendment, Act 408 will now be entitled the "Malaysia
Productivity Corporation (Incorporation) Act 1966". Clause 4 seeks to specify that
all references in any written law to the old title will be construed as reference to the
new title.


-----

_National Productivity Corporation_ 5

_(Incorporation) (Amendment)_

5. _Clause 5_ seeks to give effect to the change of name of the Corporation. The

Corporation will now be known as the "Malaysia Productivity Corporation". This
_clause_ also provides that the powers, rights, privileges, liabilities, duties and
obligations of the Corporation, and the legal proceedings by or against the
Corporation, will not be affected by the change of name of the Corporation.

6. _Clause 6_ seeks to provide that all existing liabilities incurred by the

Corporation may be enforced against the Corporation.

7. _Clause 7 deals with the vesting of the lands, property and assets of the National_

Productivity Corporation in the Malaysia Productivity Corporation.

8. _Clause 8_ seeks to provide for the transfer of moneys and property from the

National Productivity Corporation Fund to the Malaysia Productivity Corporation
Fund. A similar transfer is also provided for with regard to moneys contained in the
loan fund or any other fund of the National Productivity Corporation.

9. _Clause 9 seeks to provide that on the commencement date of the proposed Act,_

the members of the National Productivity Corporation shall be deemed to be the
members of the Malaysia Productivity Corporation.

10. _Clause 10 seeks to provide that on the commencement date of the proposed_

Act, the Committees and its members appointed by the National Productivity
Corporation shall be deemed to be the Committees and its members appointed by
the Malaysia Productivity Corporation.

11. _Clause 11 seeks to ensure the continuity of service of the officers, servants,_

agents and any other persons appointed or employed by the National Productivity
Corporation with the Malaysia Productivity Corporation from the date the proposed
Act commences.

12. Other amendments not specifically referred to in this Statement are minor or

consequential in nature.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial expenditure.
