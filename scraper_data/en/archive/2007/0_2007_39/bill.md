A BILL

_i n t i t u l e d_

An Act to apply a sum from the Consolidated Fund for the service of
the year 2008 and to appropriate that sum for the service of that year.

**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title**

**1.** This Act may be cited as the Supply Act 2008.

**Issue and appropriation for the service of the year 2008**

**2.** The issue of a sum not exceeding one hundred and five billion
five hundred and seventy-five million and one hundred and forty
thousand ringgit (RM105,575,140,000) from the Consolidated Fund
for the service of the year 2008 is hereby authorized, and that
sum is appropriated for purposes specified in the Schedule.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to provide for the issue from the Consolidated Fund a sum of one
hundred and five billion five hundred and seventy-five million one hundred and
forty thousand ringgit (RM105,575,140,000) for the service of the year 2008 and
for that sum to be appropriated for the purposes specified in the Schedule.
