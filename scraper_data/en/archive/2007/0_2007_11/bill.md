**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title and commencement**

**1.** (1)  This Act may be cited as the Penal Code (Amendment)
(Amendment) Act 2007.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed by

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill  seeks to amend the Penal Code (Amendment) Act 2006
("Act A1273").

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and provision on the commencement of_
the Act A1273.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to substitute section 7 of Act A1273 that substitute for_

section 376 of the Penal Code [Act 574] a new section 376 to insert the words
"with imprisonment" that were inadvertently omitted in the new subsection
376(1) of Act 574.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to introduce new sections 11A and 11B into Act A1273 to_
amend sections 396 and 402 of Act 574 to decrease the number of persons who
commit "gang-robbery" from five to two. These amendments are consequential
amendments to section 10 of Act A1273.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to substitute section 26 to correct the references to the_
provisions in the Penal Code (Amendment) Act 2003 [Act A1210] which has
amended section 130B of Act 574.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure.
