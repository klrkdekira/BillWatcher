_Takaful (Amendment)_

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Takaful Act 1984 ("Act 312").

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and the provision on the commencement_

of the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks insert certain expressions into section 2 of Act 312 namely_

"foreign company", "foreign institution" and "international takaful business"
and to amend the definition of "operator" to include international takaful
operator.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection 8(8) to require the registration of_

takaful operators to be published in the Gazette.

_5._ _Clause 4 seeks to amend paragraph 11(1 )(d) to provide that an international_

takaful operator which fails to comply with the requirements in the new section
40c may be subject to cancellation of its registration by the Director General
under subsection 11(1).


6. _Clause 5 seeks to introduce new Part IIA to provide for a legal framework_

for the registration of international takaful operators.

The proposed new section 40A provides the general prohibition from carrying

on business as an international takaful operator by any person unless the
requirements mentioned therein are satisfied, breach of which is an offence.

The proposed new section 40B allows a company or a foreign institution to

make an application in writing for registration to the Director General to carry
on business as an international takaful operator subject to certain licensing
requirements laid down in that section.


The proposed new section 40c provides for minimum capital funds

requirements for a company and minimum net working funds for a foreign
institution to be maintained by any person intending to be registered under
section 40B.

The proposed new section 40D provides that a takaful operator or international

takaful operator shall be subject to the provisions of Act 312 in respect of
the carrying on of the business as an international takaful operator, and any
provisions in the Act which apply to a takaful operator shall also apply to
an international takaful operator and the Minister, on the recommendation of
the Director .General, is empowered to make necessary modifications in the
application of the provisions in Act 312 to international takaful operators.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend subsection 45(4) to make that provision similarly_

applicable to international takaful operators.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to introduce new section 59A into Act 312 to empower_

the Minister, on the recommendation of the Director General, to exempt any
takaful operator, international takaful operator or person from all or any

9. _Clause 8 seeks to introduce new section 69 into Act 312 to empower the_
