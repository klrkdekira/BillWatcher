An Act to amend the Weights and Measures Act 1972.

ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:

**Short title and commencement**

**1.** (1) This Act may be cited as the Weights and Measures
(Amendment) Act 2007.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed

by the Minister by notification in the Gazette.

**Amendment of long title**

**2.** The Weights and Measures Act 1972 [Act 71], which is referred
to as "the principal Act" in this Act, is amended by substituting
for the long title the following long title:

"An Act to regulate weights and measures and instruments

for weighing and measuring and to make provisions for matters
connected therewith.".

**Amendment of section 34A**

**15.** The principal Act is amended by substituting for section 34A
the following section:

**"Compounding of offences**

**34A.** (1) The Chief Inspector may, with the consent in writing
of the Public Prosecutor, offer to compound any offence under
any provision of this Act or under regulations made under
this Act by accepting from the person reasonably suspected
of having committed the offence such amount of money not
exceeding the maximum fine to which the person would have
been liable if he had been convicted of the offence, within
such time as may be specified in the offer.

(2) An offer under subsection (1) may be made at any time

after the offence has been committed and where the amount
specified in the offer is not paid within the time specified
in the offer or such extended time as the Chief Inspector
may grant, prosecution for the offence may be instituted at
any time after that against the person to whom the offer was
made.


(3) Upon receipt of the amount under subsection (1), no

prosecution shall be instituted in respect of the offence against
the person to whom the offer to compound was made.".

_Weights and Measures (Amendment)_

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Weights and Measures Act 1972 ("Act 71")
to exclude the provisions on the establishment of units of measurement and
measurements standards as a consequence of the introduction of the National
Measurement. Standards Act 2007 ("NMSA").

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and the provisions on the commencement_

of the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend the long title of Act 71 to limit the scope of the_

Act that is to regulating weights and measures and instruments for weighing
and measuring only.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection 2(1) of Act 71. Subclauses 3(a),_

_(c) and (d) seek to amend the definitions of "instruments for weighing or_
measuring", "Minister" and "International System of Units" respectively.
_Subclauses 3(b) and (e) seek to introduce new definitions of "National_
Measurement Standards Laboratory" and "National Measurement Standards"
respectively into Act 71. Subclauses 3(f), (g) and (h) seek to delete the
definitions of "Secondary Standards", "Tertiary Standards" and "Malaysian
Primary Standards" respectively.

5. _Clauses 5 seeks to delete sections 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 and 8 of Act 71 respectively,_

since the establishment of units of measurement and measurement standards
are provided for under the NMSA.

6. _Clause 6 seeks to introduce new section 8A into Act 71. The proposed_

new section 8A seeks to provide for the traceability of any measurement made
for the purposes of Act 71 to the National Measurement Standards as set out
in the NMSA.

7. _Clause 7 seeks to amend subsection 9(2) of Act 71 so that the Working_

Standards are verified by reference to the National Measurement Standards.

8. _Clause 8 seeks to amend section 10 of Act 71 by deleting any reference to_

the Malaysian Primary Standards, Secondary Standards and Tertiary Standards
as these Standards are replaced by the National Measurement Standards which
are provided for under the NMSA.

9. _Clause 9 seeks to amend paragraph 12(1)(a) of Act 71 so that only the_

units of measurement in the First Schedule and Second Schedule to the NMSA
can be used for trade.

10. _Clause 10 seeks to amend section 25 of Act 71 by providing that the_

National Measurement Standards Laboratory shall be the Custodian of Weights
and Measures.

11. _Clause 11 seeks to amend section 25A of Act 71 so that the Custodian of_

Weights and Measures is no longer required to advise the Minister on matters
relating to the International System of Units and system of measurements.

12. _Clause 12 seeks to amend section 27A of Act 71 so as to be consistent_

with the provisions in Clause (3) of Article 145 of the Federal Constitution
on the power of the Attorney General to institute any proceedings for an
offence.

13. _Clause 13 seeks to amend subsection 29(2) of Act 71 so that no_

regulations will be made providing for matters in relation to the Malaysian
Primary Standards, Secondary Standards and Tertiary Standards.

14. _Clauses 14 seeks to delete sections 33 and 34 of Act 71 respectively,_

since the establishment of units of measurement is provided for under the
NMSA.

15. _Clause 15 seeks to amend section 34A of Act 71 so that compounding_

of offences is made only with the written consent of the Public Prosecutor.

16. _Clauses 16 and 17 seek to delete the First Schedule and the Third Schedule_

to Act 71 respectively, since the establishment of units of measurement is
provided for under the NMSA.

17. Other amendments not specifically dealt with in this Statement are minor

or consequential in nature.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill  will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure.
