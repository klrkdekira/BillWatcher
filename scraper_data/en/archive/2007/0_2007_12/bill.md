EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Criminal Procedure Code (Amendment) Act
2006 (Act A1274).

2. _Clause  1 of the Bill contains the short title and provision on the_

commencement of the proposed Act. This Act is intended to come into operation
simultaneously with Act A1274.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend paragraph 2(b) of the English language text_

of Act A1274 to replace the definition of "youth offender" with "youthful
offender".

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 4 of Act A1274 to correct the sections_

referred to in the amendment.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 5 of Act A1274. This amendment seeks_

to substitute for the words "Third Schedule" the words "Fourth Schedule"
since the deletion of the Third Schedule in the Criminal Procedure Code

[Act 593] by the Criminal Procedure Code (Amendment) Act 2002

_[Act A1132] has not come into operation._

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend section 7 of Act A1274 by renumbering section_

28A of Act 593 into subsections for ease of reference and also to correct the
usage of punctuation marks.


-----

_Bill_

7. _Clause 6 seeks to substitute section 10 of Act A1274 to correct the usage_

of the punctuation mark of full stop throughout subsection 107(3) of Act
593.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to amend section 14 of Act A1274 to insert paragraphs_

32(0 and (j) of the Evidence Act 1950 [Act 56] into subsection 113(4) of Act
593 as they were inadvertently omitted.

9. _Clause 8 seeks to amend section 27 of Act A1274 to refine the meaning_

of the "community service" in subsection 293(1) of Act 593.

10. _Clause 9 seeks to amend paragraph 33(e) of Act A1274 to insert the words_

"Fine of that have been omitted in the item relating to section 225B in the
First Schedule of Act 593 in the substitution of fine with a new penalty.

11. _Clause 10 seeks to amend section 34 of Act A1274 to substitute "Third_

Schedule" with "Fourth Schedule" as a consequential amendment to clause
_4._

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure.
