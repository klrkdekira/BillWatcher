**EXPLANATORY STATEMENT**

The object of this Bill is to give statutory authority for certain
additional payments to be made for federal public services in the
years 1992 and 1993 in excess of the expenditure sanctioned by the
Supply Act 1992, the Supplementary Supply (1991 and 1992} Act

1992. the Supplementary Supply (1991 and 1992) (No. 2) Act 1992

the Supplementary Supply (1992) Act 1992 and the Supply Act

1993.

**_FINANCIAL_** **_IMPLICATIONS_**

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial expenditure.
