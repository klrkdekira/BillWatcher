_Electricity Supply (Amendment)_ 1

A BILL

_i n t i t u l e d_

An Act to amend the Electricity Supply Act 1990.

[ ]

**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title, commencement and application**

**1.** (1) This Act may be cited as the Electricity Supply (Amendment)
Act 2015.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed
by the Minister by notification in the _Gazette, and the Minister_
may appoint different dates for the coming into operation of this
Act in different parts of Malaysia.

(3) This Act shall apply throughout Malaysia; however the
Minister may, by order, suspend the operation of the whole or
any of the provisions of this Act in any State.

**General amendment**

**2.** The Electricity Supply Act 1990 [Act 447], which is referred
to as the “principal Act” in this Act, is amended by substituting
for the words “shall be guilty of” wherever appearing the word
“commits”.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Electricity Supply Act 1990 (“Act 447”) primarily
to enhance the licensing of electricity supply activities and conduct of licensees,
improve the quality of services by licensees towards the consumers, stabilize
the regulatory and enforcement mechanism including stricter deterrent measures
to combat theft of electricity, enable the use of the latest communications
technology, advanced metering of electricity and to improve the safety of
installations and electrical equipment.

2. _Clause 1_ contains the short title and provisions on the commencement of
the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to make general amendments to Act 447 by replacing the_
words “shall be guilty of” with the word “commits”.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 447 to introduce new definitions_
and to amend existing definitions in Act 447. Amongst the new definitions
introduced are—

_(a)_ “communications” refers to electricity supply lines that may be used

for communications purposes. This is to allow electricity supply lines
to be used for communications purposes for example Broadband
on Power Lines (BPL) or Power Line Communications (PLC). The
Act currently prohibits electricity supply lines from being used for
signals or communications;


-----

48 _Bill_

_(b)_ “ring fence” refers to the separating of the positions and functions of

the System Operator and the Single Buyer from that of the licensee
for effective competition and transparency. This is to ensure that the
Single Buyer and the System Operator act independently;

_(c)_ “Single Buyer” refers to any person or a unit, department or division

forming part of a licensee who is authorized under the proposed new
subsection 22b(1) responsible for the management of procurement of
electricity and related services, which includes planning, scheduling,
procuring and settlement, and any other function as may be prescribed.
This is to ensure that the function of the Single Buyer is independent
from any activities under the licence of the licensee; and

_(d)_ “System Operator” refers to the authorized person or entity of a

licensee responsible for the operation, dispatch of electricity and
control of the power system that will be separated from any activity
of the licensee. This is to ensure that the function of the System
Operator is independent from any activities under the licence of the
licensee.
