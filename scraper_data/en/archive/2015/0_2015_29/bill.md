EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Retirement Fund Act 2007 (“Act 662”).

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title of the proposed Act and seeks to empower_
the Minister to appoint the date of commencement of the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 662 to introduce new definition_
of “debenture” and to amend certain existing definitions in Act 662.

4. _Clause 3_ seeks to introduce new section 4a into Act 662 to enable the
Retirement Fund (Incorporated) to act as an agent of the Government for the
purpose of paying any pension, gratuity or other benefit granted under any written
law from the Consolidated Fund. The Retirement Fund (Incorporated) may be
paid a sum of money which amount may be determined by the Government
of Malaysia for services rendered by it as an agent of the Government of
Malaysia.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend paragraph 6(4)(f) of Act 662 to expand the_
qualifications of a prospective Board member of the Retirement Fund
(Incorporated) to include persons who have experience and expertise in
investment or banking.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend paragraph 7(2)(b) of Act 662 to remove the_
representative from the Central Bank of Malaysia as a member of the Board
and to increase the number of persons to be appointed by the Minister under
paragraph 7(2)(e) of Act 662 from three persons to four persons who could
be appointed from the public or private sector provided they should have
experience and expertise in business, investment, banking or finance.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend subsection 13(2) of Act 662 to include moneys_
received by the Retirement Fund (Incorporated) for services rendered by it
as an agent to the Government of Malaysia under section 4a of Act 622 to
be paid into or transferred into the Retirement Fund.

8. _Clause 7_ seeks to amend subsection 14(1) Act 662 to clarify and expand
the scope of investment by the Retirement Fund (Incorporated).

9. _Clause 8 seeks to introduce a new section 21a into Act 662 to impose_
duty to maintain secrecy.

10. Other amendments not specifically dealt with in this Statement are minor
or consequential in nature.
