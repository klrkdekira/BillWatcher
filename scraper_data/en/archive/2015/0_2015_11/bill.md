An Act to amend the Civil Aviation Act 1969.

**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title and commencement**

**1.** (1) This Act may be cited as the Civil Aviation (Amendment)
Act 2015.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed
by the Minister by notification in the _Gazette._

**General amendment**

**2. The Civil Aviation Act 1969 [Act 3], which is referred to**
as the “principal Act” in this Act, is amended in the national
language text, by substituting for the words “padang terbang” the
word “aerodrom”, wherever appearing except in the definitions
of “padang terbang” and “padang terbang Kerajaan” in section 2.

air, termasuklah apa-apa lapangan terbang, lapangan

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Civil Aviation Act 1969 (“Act 3”).

1. _Clause 1 contains the short title and provision on the commencement of_
the proposed Act.


-----

10 _Bill_

2. _Clause 2 seeks to amend the national language text of Act 3 by substituting_
for the words “padang terbang” the word “aerodrom” wherever appearing
except in the definitions of “padang terbang” and “padang terbang Kerajaan”
in section 2 to be consistent with the term used in the Malaysian Aviation
Commission Act 2015.

3. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 3 to introduce certain new_
definitions and to amend certain existing definitions.

4. Clauses 4, 5, 6 and 8 seek to amend sections 3, 5a, 24a and 24k of
Act 3 respectively. These amendments are consequential upon the enactment
of the Malaysian Aviation Commission Act 2015.

_Clause 4 seeks to amend paragraph 3(2)(a) of Act 3 to empower the Minister_

to make regulations relating to the use of aircrafts on flights for hire or reward
by establishing a system of certification or otherwise in respect of technical
and safety matters.

_Clause 5_ seeks to amend section 5a of Act 3 to empower the Minister to

authorize any person to establish an aerodrome in Malaysia for the take-off
and landing of any aircraft engaged in the carriage of passengers, mail or cargo
for hire or reward and to authorize any person to establish, and license any
person to maintain or operate, an aerodrome in Malaysia for the take-off and
landing of any aircraft engaged in the carriage of passengers, mail or cargo
not for hire or reward.

_Clause 6 seeks to amend section_ 24a of Act 3 to empower the Minister to

grant a licence to a company nominated by the Government of Malaysia to
provide any services within an aerodrome or otherwise, other than the ground
handling services and operation of an aerodrome which are subject to the
Malaysian Aviation Commission Act 2015.

_Clause 8_ seeks to amend section 24k of Act 3 to substitute the words

“designated airport” with the word “aerodrome”.

5. Clause 7 seeks to amend section 24f of Act 3 to increase the fine from
one hundred thousand ringgit to five hundred thousand ringgit.

6. _Clause 9 seeks to introduce new Part VIIIb to provide for the enforcement_
and investigation and related matters.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure.
