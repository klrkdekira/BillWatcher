**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title and commencement**

**1.** (1) This Act may be cited as the Anti-Trafficking in Persons
and Anti-Smuggling of Migrants (Amendment) Act 2015.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed by
the Minister by notification in the _Gazette and the Minister may_
appoint different dates for the coming into operation of different
provisions of this Act.

in this Act, is amended in section 2 by substituting for the full
stop at the end of the definition of “premises” a semi-colon and
inserting after that definition the following definition:

‘ “place of refuge” means a place of refuge declared by the
Minister under subsection 42(1) of this Act.’.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Anti-Trafficking in Persons and Anti-Smuggling
of Migrants Act 2007 (“Act 670”).

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and provision on the commencement of_
the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 670 to introduce the definition_
of “place of refuge”.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 6 of Act 670 to add new members to the_
Council for Anti-Trafficking in Persons and Anti-Smuggling of Migrants.

5. _Clause 4_ seeks to introduce a new Part Ia relating to the High Level
Committee. This new Part Ia contains 3 new sections, namely sections 5a,
5b and 5c.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend section 7 of Act 670 to enable the Council to_
pay allowance to the trafficked person subject to the availability of funds.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend section 11 of Act 670 to provide for the Minister_
to determine the functions of the Secretary of the Council.

8. _Clause 8 seeks to amend section 43 of Act 670 to introduce subsection (1a)_
to enable the Minister to appoint any person as a Protection Officer.

9. _Clause 9 seeks to amend subsection 44(2) of Act 670 to extend the period_
of an interim protection order from fourteen days to twenty-one days.

10. _Subclause 10(b) seeks to amend subparagraph 51(3)(a)(i) of Act 670 to_
shorten the period of a Protection Order from a maximum of two years to
three months.

11. _Clause 11 seeks to introduce a new section 51a into Act 670 to empower_
the Council to grant any person permission to move freely and to work during
the period of the interim protection order or Protection Order.

12. _Clause 13 seeks to amend paragraph 54(b) of Act 670 relating to the_
release and return of a trafficked person who is a foreign national to his
country of origin.

13. _Clause 16 seeks to introduce new sections 66a and 66b into Act 670_
to enable the Court to issue an order for payment of compensation by the
convicted person to the trafficked person, and an order for payment of wages
in arrears in the case where there is no conviction to the alleged trafficked
person.

14. Other amendments not specifically dealt with in this Statement are minor
or consequential in nature.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial expenditure the amount
of which cannot at present be ascertained.
