EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Penal Code (“Act 574”).

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and provision on the commencement of_
the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to introduce new sections_ 130fa and 130fb into Act 574.
Section 130fa provides for the offence of receiving training and instruction
from terrorist groups and persons committing terrorist acts. Section 130fb
criminalises attendance at a place used for terrorist training.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection_ 130j(2) of Act 574 to extend the
categories of “support” that are prohibited by that section.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to introduce new sections_ 130ja, 130jb, 130jc and 130jd
into Act 574. Section 130ja provides for the offence of travelling to, through
or from Malaysia for the commission of terrorist acts in a foreign country or
any part of a foreign country. Section 130jb provides that it is an offence for
a person to have in his possession, custody or control any item associated with
any terrorist group or the commission of a terrorist act. The offence also covers
the act of providing, displaying, distributing or selling those items. Section 130jc
deals with the offence of building, equipping or dispatching any conveyance,
or issuing or delivering any commission for any conveyance, with intent or
knowledge or having reasonable grounds to believe that the conveyance will
be employed in or by a terrorist group to further the activities of a terrorist
group or to commit a terrorist act. Section 130jd makes it an offence for
any person to engage in any conduct in preparation for the commission of a
terrorist act or to assist another person to commit a terrorist act.
