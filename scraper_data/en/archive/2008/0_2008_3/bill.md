**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title and commencement**

1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Education (Amendment)
Act 2008.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed
by the Minister by notification in the Gazette.

**General amendments**

2. The Education Act 1996 [Act 550], which is referred to as
the "principal Act" in this Act, is amended—

_(a) by substituting for the words "Registrar General of Schools_
and Teachers" wherever appearing the words "Registrar
General of Educational Institutions and Teachers";

_(b) by substituting for the words "teacher education colleges"_
wherever appearing the words "institutes of teacher
education";

_(c) by substituting for the words "Registrar of Schools and_
Teachers" wherever appearing the words "Registrar of
Educational Institutions and Teachers";

**New section 9A**

**5. The principal Act is amended by inserting after section 9 the**
following section:

**"Change of names**

**9A. (1) All references to the "Registrar General of Schools**
and Teachers", "Deputy Registrar General of Schools and
Teachers", "Registrars of Schools and Teachers" and "Assistant
Registrars of Schools and Teachers" in any instrument,
document, agreement or working arrangement subsisting
immediately before the date of coming into operation of
this Act shall, on and after the date of coming into operation
of this Act, be construed as references to the "Registrar
General of Educational Institutions and Teachers", "Deputy
Registrar General of Educational Institutions and Teachers",
"Registrars of Educational Institutions and Teachers" and
"Assistant Registrars of Educational Institutions and Teachers"
respectively.

(2) The change of names from the "Registrar General of
Schools and Teachers", "Deputy Registrar General of Schools
and Teachers", "Registrars of Schools and Teachers" and
"Assistant Registrars of Schools and Teachers" to "Registrar
General of Educational Institutions and Teachers", "Deputy
Registrar General of Educational Institutions and Teachers",
"Registrars of Educational Institutions and Teachers" and
"Assistant Registrars of Educational Institutions and Teachers"
respectively shall not affect any powers, rights, privileges,
liabilities, duties or obligations of the "Registrar General
of Schools and Teachers", "Deputy Registrar General of
Schools and Teachers", "Registrars of Schools and Teachers"
and "Assistant Registrars of Schools and Teachers", or render
defective any legal proceeding by or against them.".

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Education Act 1996 ("Act 550").

_2._ _Clause J of the Bill contains the short title and provisions on the_
commencement of the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend Act 550 to substitute certain words namely_
"Registrar General of Schools and Teachers" with "Registrar General of
Educational Institutions and Teachers", "teacher education colleges" with
"institutes of teacher education", "Registrar of Schools and Teachers" with
"Registrar of Educational Institutions and Teachers", "Assistant Registrar of


-----

_Education_ _(Amendment)_ 5

School" with "Assistant Registrar of Educational Institutions" and "Deputy
Registrar General of Schools and Teachers" with "Deputy Registrar General
of Educational Institutions and Teachers".

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend and to insert certain expressions into section 2_
of Act 550, among others —

_(a) in relation to the definition of "special education", to clarify the_
meaning of the words "special education"; and

_(b) the insertion of the definition of "institutes of teacher education",_
which is in line with the upgrading of teacher education colleges
to institutes of teacher education.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 5 of Act 550 to insert the words "Deputy_
Registrars of Educational Institutions and Teachers" and "the Deputy Registrars"
and to substitute for the words "The Registrar or Assistant Registrar" the
words "The Registrar, the Deputy Registrar or the Assistant Registrar".

6. _Clause 5 seeks to introduce new section 9A of Act 550 as a consequent_
amendment to the change of names for the posts as specified in that
new section and the change of names shall not affect any powers, rights,
privileges, liabilities, duties or obligations of the "Registrar General of
Schools and Teachers", "Deputy Registrar General of Schools and Teachers",
"Registrars of Schools and Teachers" and "Assistant Registrars of Schools and
Teachers", or reader defective any legal proceedings by or against them.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend subsection 22(3) of Act 550 to enable the Registrar_
General to control any additional curriculum implemented in kindergartens to
be in line with the national education system and suitable with the level of
education for kindergartens.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to amend section 44 of Act 550 to enable institutes of_
teacher education to award diplomas and teaching degrees in line with the
upgrading of teacher education colleges to institutes of teacher education.

9. _Clause 8 seeks to amend section 49 of Act 550 to empower the Minister_
to make regulations with regard to the award of a teaching degree, the
establishment and management of committee for extracurricular activities
at national and international levels and procedures for the establishment of
teacher education institutes.

10. _Clause 9 seeks to amend subsection 67(1) of Act 550 to empower_
the Minister to prescribe the manner in which pupils shall be assessed and
the standards to be achieved by them at all levels of education under his
jurisdiction.

11. _Clause 10 seeks to amend subsection 77(1) of Act 550 to clarify that_
private educational institutions registered under this Act shall not conduct
any course of study or training programme with other institution of higher
education without the written approval of the Minister.
