**EXPLANATORY STATEMENT**

**This Bill seeks to amend the Labuan Offshore Financial Services Authority**
**Act 1996 "(Act 545)"'.**

_2._ _Clause 1 contains the short title and provision on the commencement of_
**the proposed Act.**

**3.** _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 545 to include an exchange_
**established under the Labuan Offshore Securities Industries Act 1998**
**(Act 579) in the definition of "offshore financial services".**


-----

**6** _Bill_

**4.** _Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection 4(5) of Act 545 to empower the_
**Authority to issue directions to an offshore financial institution in respect**
**of the action to be taken by the offshore financial institution relating to its**
**members or servants.**

**5.** _Clause 4 seeks to introduce a new section 17A into Act 545. The proposed_
**subsection 17A(1) seeks to provide that a member, officer, servant, agent or**
**consultant of the Authority or any person who has by any means access to**
**any record, book, register, correspondence, document, material or information,**
**relating to the business and affairs of the Authority in the performance of his**
**duties or the exercise of his functions, shall not give, divulge, reveal, publish**
**or otherwise disclose to any person, such record, book, register, correspondence,**
**document, material or information unless he is lawfully** **(required to do so by**
**any court or under any written law.**

**The proposed subsection 17A(2) further provides that any person who**
**contravenes subsection (1) shall be guilty of an offence and shall be liable**
**on conviction on a fine not exceeding five hundred thousand ringgit or to**
**imprisonment for a term not exceeding six months or to both.**

**6.** _Clause 5 seeks to amend section 28A of Act 545. The proposed amendment_
**seeks to amend the definitions of "domestic law enforcement agency", "criminal**
**offence" and "home supervisory authority" and to delete the definitions of**
**"financial institution" and "home monetary authority".**

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend section 28B of Act 545 to enable the Authority_
**to require an offshore financial institution or any corporation related to the**
**offshore financial institution to submit to the Authority any information—**

_(a) relating to the identity, affairs, account or particulars of any person who_
**is a customer of the offshore financial institution or any corporation**
**related to the offshore institution, or his nominee or beneficiary;**

_(b) relating to the identity, affairs or particulars of a beneficial owner_
**of an account opened with the offshore financial institution or any**
**corporation related to the offshore financial institution or relating**
**to such account; or**

_(c) which the Authority deems necessary or expedient for the performance_
**of such supervisory functions.**

**The proposed amendment also seeks to provide that the Authority may**
**disclose any information regarding the commission of fraud, money laundering**
**offence, financing terrorism offence or any criminal offence by an offshore**
**financial institution or a corporation related to the offshore financial institution**
**or any person to the home supervisory authority or domestic law enforcement**
**agency.**

**8.** **Other amendments not specifically dealt with in this Statement are minor**
**or consequential in nature.**
