A BILL

_i n t i t u l e d_

An Act to enable Malaysia to become a member of
the International Fund for Agricultural Development
by acceptance of the Agreement Establishing fhe
International Fund for Agricultural Development.

# [ ]

BE IT ENACTED by the Duli Yang Maha Mulia
Seri Paduka Baginda Yang di-Pertuan Agong with the
advice and consent of the Dewan Negara and Dewan
Rakyat in Parliament assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:

1. (1) This Act may be cited as the International Fund
for Agricultural Development Act 1991.

(2) This Act shall apply throughout Malaysia.

2. In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires—

"Agreement" means the Agreement Establishing the
International Fund for Agricultural Development as
adopted by the United Nations Conference on the
Establishment of an International Fund for Agricultural
Development on the 13th June 1976 in Rome, and
whereby a copy of the text was laid before the Dewan
Rakyat as Statute Paper No. 147 of 1991;

"freely convertible currency" means such currency
as is deemed to be freely convertib1e in accordance
with Article I(d) of the Agreement;

"Fund" means the International Fund For Agricultural
Development established by the Agreement.

6 BILL

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to enact a law to enable Malaysia to become
a member of the International Fund for Agricultural Development
by acceptance of the Agreement Establishing the International
Fund for Agricultural Development.

2. Clause 1 provides for the short title and application of this
Bill.

3. Clause 2 defines certain words and expressions used in the
Bill.

4. _Clause 3 provides for the authorization of Signature of the_
Agreement.

5. Clause 4 requires contribution to the Fund to be charged
on the Consolidated Fund.

6. Clause 5 requires any increase in the contribution to the
Fund to have prior approval of the Dewan Rakyat, signified by
a resolution.

_1. Clause 6 empowers the Minister of Finance, on behalf of_
the Government, to create and issue non-negotiable and noninterest bearing notes to carry out its obligations under the
Agreement.

8. Clause 7 requires all other payments to the Fund to be charged
on the Consolidated Fund.

9. _Clause 8 requires all receipts from the Fund to be paid into_
the Consolidated Fund.

10. _Clause 9 empowers the Yang di-Pertuan Agong to issue_
orders necessary for carrying the Agreement into effect.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial expenditure
the amount of which is yet to be determined.
