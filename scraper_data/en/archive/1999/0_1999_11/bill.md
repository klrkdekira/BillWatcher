# intituled

An Act to amend the Road Transport Act 1987.

**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title and commencement**

1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Road Transport (Amendment)
Act 1999.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed

by the Minister by notification in the Gazette, and the Minister
may appoint different dates for different provisions of this Act.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Road Transport Act 1987 ("Act 333").

2. Clause 1 seeks to provide for preliminary matters.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 333—_

_(a) to amend the definition of "Chief Police Officer";_

_(b) to introduce the definition of the term "goods vehicles";_


-----

_Road Transport (Amendment)_

_(c) to introduce the definition of the term "Kawasan Perbadanan Putrajaya";_

_(d) to introduce the definition of the term "Perbadanan Putrajaya"; and_

_(e) to amend the definition of "traffic warden"._

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 3 of Act 333 to enable the Perbadanan_

Putrajaya to exercise the powers conferred and to perform the duties imposed
by the Act. Similar amendments are also proposed in clauses 4, 18, 19, 21,
_23, 26, 28 and 29 to sections 4, 63, 65, 67, 113, 117, 120 and 123._

5. Clauses 7, 8, 9, 10, I I , 12, 13 and 14 seek to amend sections 41, 42, 43,
44, 45, 45A, 45B and 45c of Act 333 respectively to increase the penalties for
offences committed under those sections.

6. _Clause 15 seeks to amend section 45D of Act 333 to provide that only_

blood or urine samples are to be sent for laboratory test and that the medical
practitioner referred to in that section is a registered medical practitioner.

7. _Clauses 16 and 17 seek to amend sections 45F and 45G of Act 333 respectively_

to enable blood samples to be taken from any person without the consent of
that person.

8. _Subclauses I9(a), (b)(ii) and (f) seek to amend section 65 of Act 333 to_

empower a concession company duly authorized in writing by the Director
General of Highway Authority Malaysia to remove broken down or abandoned
motor vehicles and to be paid a prescribed fee for such services.

9. _Clause 20 seeks to amend section 66 of Act 333 to empower the Minister_

to make rules to prescribe any substance as a drug within the meaning and
for the purposes of Act 333.

10. _Clause 22 seeks to amend section 109 of Act 333 to provide that where_

a licence holder is the registered owner of a motor vehicle used by any of
his employees in the course of his employment and the motor vehicle is used
in the commission of an offence under the Act, the licence holder shall be
guilty of an offence under that section.

11. _Clause 24 seeks to delete section 116 of Act 333 as under section 377_

of the Criminal Procedure Code the power to appoint the person who may
prosecute for an offence is vested in the Public Prosecutor.

12. _Clause 25 seeks to introduce new section 116A into Act 333 to confer_

a jurisdiction on a First Class Magistrate to impose the maximum punishment
as proposed under this Act notwithstanding that subsection 87(1) of the
Subordinate Courts Act 1948 permits a Magistrate to only pass a sentence not
exceeding a fine of ten thousand ringgit and imprisonment for a term not
exceeding five years.

13. _Clause 27 seeks to amend section 119 of Act 333 to increase the maximum_

penalties for general offences under Act 333.
