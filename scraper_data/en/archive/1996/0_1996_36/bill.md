EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Income Tax Act 1967, the Petroleum
(Income Tax) Act 1967, the Real Property Gains Tax Act 1976, the
Labuan Offshore Business Activity Tax Act 1990, the Service Tax
Act 1975, the Sales Tax Act 1972, the Customs Act 1967 and the
Free Zones Act 1990, and to repeal the Cinematograph Film-Hire
Duty Act 1965.
