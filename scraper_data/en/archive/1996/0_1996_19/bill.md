This Bill seeks to amend the Rubber Industry Smallholders
Development Authority Act 1972 ("the Act").

2. Clause 1 seeks to provide for preliminary matters.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act by inserting a_
new subsection (1).

4. Clause 3 seeks to introduce new sections 11B, 11C, 11D, 11E,
11 F,11 G, 11H. 11I, 11J and 11 K . The proposed new sections 11 B and
11C seek to provide for the establishment of a Personnel Committee
and a Personnel Appeal Committee respectively. The proposed new
section 11D seeks to provide for the establishment of disciplinary
committees for the different categories of officers and servants of
the Authority. The proposed new section 11E seeks to allow an
appeal to be made to the Minister or Disciplinary Appeal Committee,
as the case may be, against the decision of the disciplinary committee.
The proposed new section 11F seeks to empower the Authority,


-----

with the approval of the Minister, to make regulations to provide
for disciplinary offences and punishments. The proposed new sections
11G, 11H, 11I and 11J relate to the imposition of surcharge on any
person who is or was in the employment of the Authority. The
proposed new section 1 1 K seeks to provide that in any action for
surcharge against the Director General, the composition of the
Authority for the purpose of sections 11G and 11J shall not include
the Director General.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure.
