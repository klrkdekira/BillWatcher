EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Lembaga Kemajuan Pahang Tenggara
Act 1972 ("the Act").

2. Clause 1 seeks to provide for preliminary matters.

3. Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act by inserting the
definition of "accounting officer".

4. Clause 3 seeks to introduce new sections 16A, 16B, 16c, 16D,
16E, 16F, 16G and 16H. The proposed new section 16A seeks to
provide for the establishment of disciplinary committees for the

different categories of officers or servants of the Lembaga. The
proposed new section 16B seeks to allow an appeal to be made to
the Minister or the Disciplinary Appeal Committee, as the case
may be, against the decision of the disciplinary committee. The
proposed new section 16c seeks to empower the Lembaga, with the
approval of the Minister, to make regulations to provide for disciplinary
offences and punishments. The new sections 16D, 16E, 16F and 16G
relate to the imposition of surcharge on any person who is or was
in the employment of the Lembaga. The proposed new section 16H
seeks to provide that in any action for surcharge against the General
Manager the composition of the Lembaga for the purpose of sections
16D and 16G shall not include the General Manager.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure.
