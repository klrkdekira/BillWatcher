Companies Act 1990.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend subsection 7(3) of the Act to stipulate_
when an offshore company can carry on business in Malaysian
currency and with Malaysian residents and to allow petroleum activities
to be conducted by offshore companies. Clause 4 also seeks to
amend subsection 7(4) to clarify instances when an offshore company
is deemed not to be dealing with residents.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend subsection 9(2) to provide for instances_
when documents could be extracted from or lodged with the Registrar
without the need to go through a trust company.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend section 21 to require that certain labels_
form part of the name of an offshore company.

8. Clause 7 seeks to amend subsection 29(2) to permit residents
to subscribe for shares in an offshore company but cannot subscribe
for debentures or lend to or deposit money with an offshore company.

9. Clause 8 seeks to amend section 51 to allow redenomination
of shares from one currency to another.

10. Clause 13 seeks to insert a new subsection (2A) into section
86 and a new paragraph (d) into subsection (4) in that section.
Under the proposed provisions the company number must appear
on the memorandum and articles of association of an offshore company
and on other prescribed documents.

11. _Clause 14 seeks to amend subsection 87(2) to allow a domestic_
company wholly owned by a trust company to be a director of an
offshore company.

12. _Clause 15 seeks to amend section 93 to insert a new subsection_
(1A) and to amend subsection (2) to allow a corporation to be a
secretary to an offshore company.

13. _Clause 16 seeks to amend subsections 94(2) and 94(3) to set_
out the particulars of a director and secretary of any offshore company
that must be entered in the register.

14. _Clause 17 seeks to insert a new section 94A to set out the_
liability of corporate directors or secretaries for, and defences to
offences against the Act.

## 22 Bill

15. _Clause 21 seeks to introduce a new Part, namely Part VIIIA,_
into the Act to provide for the licensing, control, administration
and liabilities of management companies.

16. _Clause 23 seeks to amend section 147 to allow offshore_
companies owned by non-residents to hold securities in a domestic
company subject to the limitation that it does not amount to a
controlling interest in the domestic company.

17. Clause 24 seeks to amend section 150 to widen the scope of
persons on whom the Minister may grant exemption from certain
provisions of the Act.

18. _Clause 25 seeks to amend section 151 and clause 26 seeks_
to insert new sections 15lA to 151c to provide for circumstances
under which the names of offshore companies may be struck off
the register and the consequences of such striking off.

19. Other amendments not specifically mentioned in this Statement
are—

_(a) amendments to several provisions to lengthen the period_
for doing certain acts under the Act from a week or two
weeks to a month; and

_(b) consequential and minor amendments._
