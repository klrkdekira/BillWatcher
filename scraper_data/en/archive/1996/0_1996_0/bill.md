EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to create a council to be known as the "National
Council on Higher Education" and to provide for its functions relating
to higher education in Malaysia.

2. Part I deals with preliminary matters.

_Clause 1 contains the short title and provision on the_

commencement of the proposed Act.

_Clause 2 defines certain words and expressions used in this Bill._

3. Part II contains provisions relating to the Nationai Council on
Higher Education ("the Council").

_Clause 3 establishes the Council._

_Clause 4 seeks to provide for the membership of the Council._

_Clause 5 requires the Secretary General of the Ministry of Education_

to chair the meeting of the Council in the absence of the Chairman.

_Clause 6 deals with the appointment and duration of appointment_

of the members of the Council under paragraphs 4(g), (h) and (i).


-----

10 BILL

_Clause 7 spells out the circumstances under which a person is_

disqualified from being a member of the Council.

In clause 8, a member appointed under paragraph 4 _(g), (h) or_

_(i) shall cease to be a member if he fails to attend three consecutive_
meetings of the Council without leave of the Chairman of the Council
or if his appointment is revoked.

_Clause 9 seeks to empower the Minister to appoint another person_

in the event of a vacancy among the members of the Council appointed
under paragraph 4 _(g), (h) or (i)._

A member of the Council appointed under paragraph 4(i) may

resign as a member of the Council by giving a month's notice in
writing to the Minister under clause 10.

_Clause 11 deals with the termination of appointment of a member_

of Ihe Council appointed under paragraph 4 _(g), (h) or (i)._

_Clause 12 seeks to provide for the functions of the Council. The_

primary role of the Council is to plan, formulate and determine
national policies and strategies and set guidelines on matters pertaining
to higher education in Malaysia as enumerated in paragraphs (l)(a)
to (k).

4. Part III contains general provisions.

_Clause 13 seeks to empower the Council to establish committees_

to assist it in discharging its functions under the proposed Act.

_Clause 14 seeks to provide that the Council shall determine its_

own procedure.

_Clause 15 deals with the meetings of the Council._

_Clause 16 seeks to require a member who has any beneficial_

interest in any matter under consideration by the Council to disclose
such interest to the Chairman of the Council.

_Clause 17 seeks to require the Council to cause minutes of all_

its meetings to be maintained and kept in a proper form.

_Clause 18 provides for the payment of allowances to the members_

of the Council or their representatives for attending meetings of the
Council.

_Clause 19 seeks to provide grants-in-aid to the Council to enable_

it to carry out its functions under the proposed Act.
