EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Employees Provident Fund Act 1991
("the Act").

2. _Clause 1 deals with the short title and the date of commencement_
of the Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend the Act by substituting the words_
"General Manager" with the words "chief executive officer". It also
seeks to delete the word "Deputy General Manager".

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act by providing a_
definition of the words "chief executive officer".

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend paragraph 26A(2)(c) to empower the_
Minister to vary any limitation imposed on the total amount of the
borrowings of the approved company under the said paragraph.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend subsection 26B(3) by deleting paragraph_
_(d). With this amendment, bonds having a maturity period of not_
less than two years would no longer be regarded as securities issued
by the Government.

7. Clause 6 seeks to introduce a new section 26c to empower the
Board to establish a corporation or a company with the special
approval of the Minister.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to introduce a new Part IIIA in order to allow_
members of the Fund to invest the amount standing to their credit
in fund management institutions approved by the Minister.


-----

EMPLOYEES PROVIDENT FUND (AMENDMENT) 11

9. _Clause 8 seeks to amend section 30 to provide flexibility to_
the Board to determine the designation of the chief executive officer
and to allow the Board to appoint such number of other oficers or
servants as it deems appropriate.

10. Clause 9 seeks to amend section 31 to establish a Disciplinary
Committee in respect of officers and servants of the Board.

11. _Clause 10 seeks to amend section 33 to establish a Promotion_
Committee in respect of officers and servants of the Board.

12. _Clause 11 seeks to amend the proviso to subsection 45(3) to_
provide that the rate of last dividend declared shall apply for the
purpose of calculating dividend for that year where the rate of
dividend has not been declared by the Board.

13. _Clause 12 seeks to amend section 50 to clarify that dividend_
shall only be payable on any amount standing to the credit of a
member and to delete the proviso to subsection (2) and subsection
(5).

14. _Clause 13 seeks to amend subsection 54(2) to limit the number_
of elections by a member who has made full withdrawal to recontribute
and to make applicable the provisions of section 54B in determining
the date of withdrawal of payments.

15. Clause 14 seeks to introduce new sections 54A and 54B. Section
54A seeks to provide that the payment of dividend shall be in such
manner as determined by the Board from time to time. Section 54B
seeks to clarify the determination of the date of withdrawal for
payment made by a warrant and for payment made in a manner
other than by a warrant.

16. _Clause 15 seeks to introduce a new section 59A to provide_
that the court shall order such person who is found guilty of an
offence under subsection 59 (b) to produce any declaration or document
required by the Act to be furnished or produced.

17. Clause 16 seeks to amend section 71 to empower the Minister
to prescribe fees or charges which shall be paid to the Board in
respect of services provided by the Board and to make regulations
in respect of investment under Part IIIA.

18. _Clause 17 seeks to amend section 73 to empower the Board_
to make rules in respect of investment under Part IIIA.

19. _Clause 18 seeks to amend paragraph (5) of the First Schedule_
so as to exclude persons detained in drug rehabilitation centres
from the definition of employees under the Act and, to amend
paragraphs (6), (7) and (8) of the First Schedule as references to
the Republic of Singapore are no longer necessary.

20. _Clause 19 seeks to amend the Fifth Schedule to provide that_
the amount invested under the new Part IIIA of the Act shall not
be included as part of the credit of a member for the purpose of
calculating additional amount in the event of death.

21. _Clause 20 seeks to amend the Sixth Schedule to provide that_
the amount invested under the new Part IIIA of the Act shall not
be included as part of the credit of a member for the purpose of
calculating additional amount in the event of incapacity.

22. _Clause 21 seeks to insert a new Seventh Schedule into the_
Act which specifies the provisions which are applicable only to a
corporation established by the Board.
