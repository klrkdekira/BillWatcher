EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Malaysian Rubber Exchange
(Incorporation) Act 1962 ("the Act").

2. Clause 1 deals with preliminary matters, including the Minister's
power to appoint the date for the coming into force of this Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act to substitute_
certain definitions and to introduce certain new definitions for
expressions used in the Act.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection 3(4) of the Act to substitute_
for the word "Director" the word "person".

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 5 of the Act to provide that_
the income of the Corporation is to accrue to the Board.

6. Clause 6 seeks to amend section 7 of the Act to introduce a
new category of membership of the Corporation and to remove the
restriction on membership.

7. Clause 7 seeks to delete section 8 of the Act to remove the
exclusive rights of Ordinary Members.

8. Clause 8 seeks to amend section 12 of the Act to provide for
the delegation of the management powers of the Board to the
Management Committee established under section 17 of the Malaysian
Rubber Board (Incorporation) Act 1996, the power to appoint, with
the approval of the Minister, a Chairman of the Corporation, and
the power to appoint a Deputy Chairman.
