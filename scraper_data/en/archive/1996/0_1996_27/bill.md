An Act to amend the Institut Teknologi MARA Act 1976.

# [              ]

BE IT ENACTED by the Seri Paduka Baginda Yang
di-Pertuan Agong with the advice and consent of the
Dewan Negara and Dewan Rakyat in Parliament
assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:

**1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Institut Teknologi**
MARA (Amendment) Act 1996.

(2) This Act shall come into force on a date to be
appointed by the Minister by notification in the Gazette,

provisions of this Act.

the date of coming into force of this Act appointed under
subsection 1(2).

appearing therein the word "Provost".

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Institut Teknologi MARA Act 1976 (the
"Act").

1. Clause 1 contains the short title and provisions on the commencement

of the Act.

2. Clause 2 seeks to define the term "appointed date" to mean the date
of coming into force of the Act.

3. Clause 3 seeks to make general amendments to the Act, The words
"Director" has been substituted for the word "Rector", "Majlis" for the
words "Board of Directors" and "Principal" for the word "Provost".

4. _Clause 4 seeks to give effect to the change of executive body of the_
Institut from the Majlis to the Board of Directors and to provide that all
references to the old executive body are to be construed as references to the
new executive body.

_Subclauses4(2) and (3) relate to the rights, privileges, liabilities, duties_
or obligations of the Majlis, and legal proceedings by or against it, which
are not affected by the change of executive body.

5. Clause 5 seeks to provide that all existing liabilities incurred by the
Majlis may be enforced against the Board of Directors.

6. Clause 6 seeks to provide that all land, property and assets vested in
the Majlis will, after the date of coming into force of the Act, vest in the
Board of Directors.

7. _Clause 7 seeks to provide that any reference to the Director or_
Principal will, after the date of coming into force of the Act, be construed
as references to the Rector or Provost respectively.


-----

46 BILL

_Subclauses 7(2) and (3) relate to the rights, privileges, liabilities, duties_
or obligations of the Director or the Principal, and legal proceedings by or
against them, which are not affected by the change of designation.

8. Clause 8 seeks to provide that all existing liabilities incurred by the
Director or Principal may be enforced against the Rector or Provost
respectively.

9. Clause 9 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act to introduce certain
new definitions and to amend certain definitions used in the Act, the more
significant being as follows:

_(a) the definition of "Board" to refer to the Board of Director of_
the Institut as the new executive body of the Institut in place
of the Majlis;

_(b) the definition of "degree" as the Institut is empowered to_
confer degrees;

_(c) the definition of "Rector" as the chief executive, admini-_
strative and academic officer of the Institut who may also be
styled as the "President" of the Institut;

_(d) the definition of "School or Centre" to include Faculty,_
College, Department and Academy of the Institut;

_(e) the definition of "staff to specifically reflect the appoint-_
ment of lecturers and to include senior professors, professors,
_etc.; and_

_(f) the definitions of "student" and "Students' Representative_
Committee" ("SRC"), all for the purposes of the provisions of
the Act relating to students of the Institut.

10. _Clause 10 seeks to make general amendments to the national_
language text of the Act by substituting for the words "Meteri", "meteri"
and "dimeterikan" the words "Meterai", "meterai" and "dimeteraikan"
respectively and for the word "pelajaran" the word "pendidikan".

11. _Clause 11 seeks to amend section 4 of the Act to empower the_
Institut, amongst others, to confer degrees, honorary degrees or awards
and to establish Branches outside Malaysia. It also seeks to accord the
status of degrees at Bachelor level, with or without honours, as the case
may be, the Diplomas and Advanced Diplomas which were conferred by
the Institut and have been recognised by the Government to be equivalent
to degrees, from the dates the Government gave its recognition.

12. _Clause 12 seeks to amend the Act to introduce a new section 4A_
which confers additional powers on the Institut. Amongst others, it
includes power to borrow money, to enter into business joint-ventures, to
establish companies and corporations, and to acquire and hold for investment
shares, etc. Where a corporation is established by the Institut, it shall
adhere to the provisions of the new First Schedule to the Act.

13. Clause 13 seeks to amend section 5 of the Act to enable a principal
officer of the Institut or a representative of the Rector, as the circumstances

ere., in place of the Rector.

14. _Clause 14 seeks to amend section 6 of the Act to specify that the_
main campus of the Institut is at Shah Alam, in the State of Selangor.

15. _Clause 15 seeks to amend section 7 of the Act to allow the Institut_
to establish and maintain its Branch or Branches abroad.

16. _Clause 16 seeks to amend section 8 of the Act to delete the_

matters of the Institut and no resolution affecting any such matter can be
passed by the Board unless the Senate has first been given the opportunity
of recording and transmitting to the Board its opinion thereon.

21. _Clause 23 seeks to amend section 21 of the Act to clarify that the_

approved by the Board of Directors instead of the Minister.

22. Clause 25 seeks to amend the Act to introduce a new section 22A to
provide for the appointment of other principal officers of the Institut.

23. Clause 27 seeks to amend the Act to introduce new section 23A
relating to the discipline of the staff of the Institut.

At present, the disciplinary authority in respect of the staff is the
Disciplinary Committee of the Institut constituted under subsection 4( 1),
or any member of the staff of the Institut or any board of members of the
staff of the Institut to whom disciplinary functions have been delegated
under subsection 4(3), of the Educational Institutions (Discipline) Act
1976 ("Act 174"). The new section 23A provides for a change of the
disciplinary authority. It empowers the Board of Directors to appoint
different disciplinary committees in respect of different categories of staff.

24. _Clause 28 seeks to provide for transitional provisions relating to_
disciplinary proceedings which are pending before, and appeals against
the decision of, the Disciplinary Committee of the Institut or any member
of the staffer any board of members of the staff of the Institut as explained
in paragraph 23. These provisions are necessitated by the change of
disciplinary authority over the staff.

25. _Clause 29 contains transitional provisions relating to the existing_
disciplinary rules made under section 35 of the Act.

26. Act 174 also contains provisions for matters relating to students of
the Institut. With the view to enable the Institut to specifically deal with
such matters under the Act, clause 30 seeks to create a new Part IVA
containing 11 new sections, namely, sections 23B to 23L which substantially
incorporate the provisions of Parts II, III and IV of Act 174, and certain
provisions of Act 30, as may be necessary for the purposes of this Act.

Necessary amendments will have to be made to the First Schedule to
Act 174 simultaneously upon the coming into force of the new section 23A
and the new Part IVA.

27. _Clause 31 seeks to amend section 24 of the Act to add "subventions"_
and "leases" to the Institut's Fund whilst the proposed amendment to
section 28 of the Act by clause 32 would enable the Board of Directors to
accept "subventions" and "legacies" in aid of the Institut's finances.

28. Clause 33 seeks to amend the Act to introduce a new section 28A
which relates to the imposition of surcharge on past or present staff of the
Institut with regard to any improper payment, deficiency in or destruction

delay in making payment, where no satisfactory explanation for such
action has been given. The amount of such surcharge shall be a debt due
to the Institut. .

29. _Clause 34 seeks to amend section 29 of the Act to require the_

with the national policies, strategies and guidelines on higher education
formulated or determined by the National Council on Higher Education.

30. Clause 35 seeks to amend the Act to introduce new section 29A. New
section 29A seeks to empower the Minister to delegate to the Board of
Directors any of his powers, duties or functions under the Act.

31. Clause 36 seeks to amend the Act to introduce new sections 34A and
34B. The new section 34A seeks to protect any actor omission of the Board
of Directors, member of the Board, the Rector, the Deputy Rector, the
Senate, member of the Senate, staff of the Institut or any person authorised
by the Institut, done in good faith. The new section 34B contains provisions
relating to service of document.

32. _Clause 37 seeks to amend section 35 of the Act to empower the_
Board of Directors to make rules instead of the Minister. It includes the
power to make rules pertaining to the award of degrees, the discipline of
the staff and students of the Institut and any matters relating to the SRC and
other student bodies.

33. Clause 38 seeks to introduce new First, Second and Third Schedules
to the Act.

The First Schedule contains provisions relating to corporations
established by the Institut.

The Second Schedule contains provisions regulating the Board, the
committees and the Senate.

The Third Schedule contains provisions relating to the SRC and other
student bodies.

34. Other amendments not specifically referred to are minor or
consequential in nature.
