# intituled

## An Act to amend the Lembaga Kemajuan Wilayah Jengka

Act 1983.

## [ ]

 BE IT ENACTED by the Seri Paduka Baginda Yang
 di-Pertuan Agong with the advice and consent of the
Dewan Negara and Dewan Rakyat in Parliament assembled,
and by the authority of the same, as follows:

## 1. This Act may be cited as the Lembaga Kemajuan
 Wilayah Jengka (Amendment) Act 1996.

 2. The Lembaga Kemajuan Wilayah Jengka Act 1983, which in this Act is referred to as the "principal Act", is amended in section 2 by inserting before the definition of "Chairman" the following definition:

 ' "accounting officer" includes every officer and
 servant who is charged with the duty of collecting, receiving, or accounting for, or who in fact collects, receives or accounts for, any money of the Fund, or who is charged with the duty of disbursing, or who does in fact disburse, any such money, and every officer and servant who is charged with the receipt, custody or disposal of, or the accounting for, any store and property of the Lembaga or who in fact receives, holds or disposes of such store and property;'.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Lembaga Kemajuan Wilayah Jengka
Act 1983 ("the Act").

2. Clause 1 seeks to provide for preliminary matters.

3. Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act by inserting a
definition for the word "accounting officer".

4. Clause 3 seeks to introduce new sections 17A, 17B, 17c, 17D,
17E, 17F, 17G and 17H. The proposed new section 17 A seeks to
provide for the establishment of disciplinary committees for different
categories of officers and servants of the Lembaga. The proposed
new section 17B seeks to allow an appeal to be made to the Minister
or the Disciplinary Appeal Committee, as the case may be, against
the decision of the disciplinary committee. The proposed new section
17c seeks to empower the Lembaga, with the approval of the Minister,
to make regulations to provide for disciplinary offences and
punishments. The new sections 17D, 17E, 17F and 17G relate to the
imposition of surcharge on any person who is or was in the employment
of the Lembaga. The proposed new section 17 H seeks to provide that
in any action for surcharge against the General Manager, the
composition of the Lembaga for the purpose of sections 17D and 17G
shall not include the General Manager.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure.
