_i n t i t u l e_

An Act to amend the Child Care Centre Act 1984.

**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title and commencement**

**1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Child Care Centre (Amendment)**
Act 2006.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed by

the Minister by notification in the Gazette.

**Amendment of section 2**

**2. The Child Care Centre Act 1984 [Act 308], which is referred**
to as the "principal Act" in this Act, is amended in section 2—

_(a)_ by inserting after the definition of "relative" the following

definition:

' "State Director" means the Director of Social Welfare

of a State and includes the Director of Social Welfare of
the Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur, the Federal Territory
of Labuan and the Federal Territory of Putrajaya
respectively.'; and

_(b)_ in the English language text, by substituting for the full

stop at the end of the definition of "relative" a semicolon.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT This Bill seeks to

amend the Child Care Centre Act 1984 ("Act 308")
2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and provision on the commencement of_
the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 308 to provide for a new definition_
of "State Director" which refers to the Director of Social Welfare of a State
and includes the Director of Social Welfare of the Federal Territory of Kuala
Lumpur, the Director of Social Welfare of the Federal Territory of Labuan and
the Director of Social Welfare of the Federal Territory of Putrajaya.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 3 of Act 308 to exempt any child care_
centre which is not only run but also established by the Federal Government
or a State Government from the provisions of this Act.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 5 of Act 308 to introduce two new_
categories of child care centres namely a child care centre at work place and
a community based child care centre.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend subsection 6(2) of Act 308 to enhance the penalty_
for the offence of operating a child care centre without registration.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend subsection 11(3) of Act 308 to extend the period_
of validity of the certificate of registration from twelve months to sixty months.
The requirement to exhibit the certificate of registration is provided in subsection
11(5).  Subsection 11(6) provides for the surrender of the certificate upon the
closing down of a child care centre.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to introduce a new section 1 3 A into Act 308 which enables_
the State Director of Social Welfare to carry out duties of registration of child
care centres.


-----

8 _Bill_

_9._ _Clause 8 seeks to amend paragraph \4(e) of the national language text of_

Act 308 by substituting the word "api" the words "bahaya kebakaran".

10. _Clause 9 seeks to amend section 17 of Act 308 to make it an offence for_

failing to notify the Director General of any change that occurs in any of the
circumstances particulars of which are specified in the certificate of registration,
failing to exhibit the certificate of registration or failing to surrender the certificate
upon closing down of the child care centre. Clause 9 also seeks to enhance the
penalty for the offences under this section.

11. _Clause 10 seeks to introduce a new section 17A into Act 308 pertaining_

to offences committed by body corporate and by employees and agents.

12. _Clause 11 seeks to introduce a new section 20A into Act 308 to empower_

the Director General and authorized officers to seal unregistered child care
centres.

13. _Clause 12 seeks to amend section 21 of Act 308 pertaining to prosecution_

to make it consistent with Article 145 of the Federal Constitution.

14. _Clause 13 seeks to introduce a new section 21A into Act 308 to enable_

any offence committed under this Act or any regulations made under this Act
to be compounded.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial expenditure the amount
of which cannot at present be ascertained.
