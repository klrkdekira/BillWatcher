EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Population and Family Development Act 1966
("Act 352").

2. Clause 1 contains the short title and the commencement of the proposed
Act.


-----

20 _Bill_

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 352 to introduce the definitions_
of "Board", "Chairman", "Deputy Chairman" and "Fund".

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 3 of Act 352 to state specifically that_
the Board may, in respect of movable and immovable property and interest in
movable and immovable property, of every description, acquire, purchase and
take such property and interest for public or social purposes and hold, enjoy,
convey, assign, surrender, yield up, charge, mortgage, demise, reassign, transfer
or otherwise dispose of, or deal with, such property and interest vested in the
Board. This clause also seeks to amend subsection 3(2) of Act 352 by inserting
the word "First" before the word "Schedule" in the said subsection.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to introduce new sections 3A,_ 3B and 3c into Act 352
to provide for more specific provisions on the functions and powers of the
Board.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend section 4 of Act 352 namely, to include a Deputy_
Chairman and the Secretary General of the Ministry responsible for population
and family development or his representative as members of the Board and to
provide for the appointment by the Minister of not more than nine members
from amongst members of the public and non-governmental organizations who
possess expertise in the areas of population, family development and human
reproduction.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to introduce new sections 4A,_ 4B, 4C, 4D, 4E, 4F and 4G
into Act 352. New section 4A seeks to provide for the tenure of office of a
member of the Board appointed under paragraphs 4(a) and 4(e). The proposed
section 4B seeks amongst others, to empower the Minister, in respect of each
member appointed under paragraphs 4(d) and (e), to appoint an alternate
member to attend, in place of that member, meetings of the Board that that
member is for any reason unable to attend. The proposed section 4c provides
for the composition of the Executive Committee of the Board. The proposed
section 4D provides for the temporary exercise of the functions of Chairman
by the Deputy Chairman where the Chairman is for any reason unable to
perform the functions of the Chairman or during any period of vacancy in
the office of the Chairman. The proposed section 4E empowers the Minister
to revoke the appointment of any member at any time without assigning any
reason and also provides that a member of the Board may at any time resign
his office by a written notice to the Minister. The proposed section 4F lists
the circumstances where the office of a member of the Board shall be vacated.
The proposed section 4G seeks to give protection to any member of the Board
from incurring any personal liability for any loss or damage caused by an
act in administering the affairs of the Board, unless the loss or damage is
occasioned by an intentionally wrongful act on his part.

8. _Clause 8 seeks to amend section 6 of Act 352 to empower the Board_
to delegate any of its powers under paragraphs 3B(2)(a), (c) and (d) and
paragraph 7A(1)(b) of Act 352 to the Executive Committee of the Board. It
also seeks to provide that the Board may delegate to the Director General the
power to grant loans and make advances to the officers and servants of the
Board and the authority to sanction expenditure from the Fund or any other
moneys vested in or under the control of the Board up to such limit as the
Board shall specify.

_Population and Family Development (Amendment)    21_

9. Clause 10 seeks to amend section 7 of Act 352 to empower the Minister to
appoint a fit and proper person with the relevant experience and expertise as
a Director General of the Board, on such terms and conditions as the
Minister may determine. It also sets out the responsibilities of the Director
General, namely, to execute government policies pertaining to population,
family development and human reproduction, and any other policy as the Board
deems fit. It also seeks to empower the Board to direct the Deputy Director
General to perform the duties of the Director General if the Director General
is temporarily absent from Malaysia or is temporarily incapacitated through
illness or for any other reason from performing his duties or if the office of
the Director General is for the time being vacant.

10. _Clause 11 seeks to introduce a new section 7A into Act 352 to empower_

the Board with the approval of the Minister, to appoint such number of Deputy
Directors General, officers and servants of the Board. It also seeks to empower
the Board, with the approval of the Minister, to make regulations with respect
to the conditions of service of its officers and servants.

11. _Clause 13 seeks to amend section 8 of Act 352 to provide for the_

establishment of a Fund and to list out the composition of the Fund.

12. _Clause 14 seeks to introduce a new section 8A into Act 352 to specify_

the expenditure to be charged on the Fund.

13. _Clause 15 seeks to introduce new sections 9A,_ 9B and 9c into

Act 352 to provide that the Statutory Bodies (Accounts and Annual Reports)
Act 1980 [Act 240] applies to the Board and to any corporation established under
Act 352. It also seeks to bring the provisions on annual estimates and annual
report into Part III of Act 352.

14. _Clause 16 seeks to substitute Part IV of Act 352 with provisions dealing_

with the other powers and functions of the Board such as the power to borrow,
power to invest, power to make grants out of Parliamentary appropriations
for purposes related to the Board's functions and also power to employ and
pay agents, etc.

15. _Clause 17 seeks to introduce new Part V into Act 352. Amongst others, it_

states in greater detail the regulations that the Minister is empowered to make
under Act 352. It also provides for the applicability of the Public Authorities
Protection Act 1948 [Act 198] to any action, suit, prosecution or proceedings
against the Board or against any member, officer, servant or agent of the
Board in respect of any act, neglect or default done or committed by him in
such capacity. The proposed section 18 empowers the Minister to amend, add
to or vary the Schedules to Act 352 by order published in the Gazette.

16. _Clause 19 seeks to introduce a new Second Schedule to Act 352 that_

deals with matters relating to corporations established under Act 352.

17. _Clause 20 seeks to ensure the continuity of appointment of the existing_

Board members and Director General and the continuity of service of the
officers and servants of the Board from the date the proposed Act come into
operation.
