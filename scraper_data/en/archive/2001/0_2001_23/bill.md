EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Patents Act 1983 ("Act 291"). consequent upon
the establishment of the Intellectual Property Corporation of Malaysia under the
proposed Intellectual Property Corporation of Malaysia Act 2001.

2. Clause J contains the short title and provisions on the commencement of
the proposed amendment Act.

3. Clause 2 seeks to amend section 3 of Act 291 to amend certain existing
definitions and to introduce certain new definitions.

4. Clause 3 seeks to delete Part II of Act 291 to remove all references to the
Patents Board. It is proposed that the Board's function be taken over by the
Corporation.

5. Clause 4 seeks to amend section 8 of Act 291 to provide, inter alia, that
the Director General of the Intellectual Property Corporation of Malaysia shall
be the Registrar of Patents. The clause also seeks to empower the Corporation

to appoint its employees to be Deputy Registrars, Assistant Registrars and other
officers of Patents. The Deputy Registrars, Assistant Registrars and other officers
of Patents who have opted to be employees of the Corporation on the appointed
date shall continue to be the Deputy Registrars, Assistant Registrars and other
officers of Patents, as the case may be, on and after the appointed date.

6. Clause 13 provides that a prosecution under Act 291 can only be instituted
by or with the prior consent in writing of the Public Prosecutor.

7. _Clause 17 contains saving provision._

8. Other amendments not specifically referred to in this Statement are minor

or consequential in nature.
