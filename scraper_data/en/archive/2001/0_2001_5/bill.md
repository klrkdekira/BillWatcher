EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Free Zones Act 1990 ("Act 438").

2. _Clause 1 of the Bill contains the short title._

3. _Clause 2 of the Bill seeks to introduce a new section 23A into Act 438 to_
enable an officer of customs exercising his powers under sections 21, 22 and
23 of Act 438 to gain access to recorded information or computerised data.

4. _Clause 4 of the Bill seeks to amend subsection 27(1) of Act 438 to allow_
the amount secured or the proceeds of sale held under that section to be paid
to the Director-General if such amount or proceeds of sale are forfeited under
section 33 of Act 438.

5. _Clause 5 of the Bill seeks to amend section 28 of Act 438 to provide for_
the release of a person arrested under Act 438 from custody on his depositing
such reasonable sum of money as the proper officer of customs may require
or on his executing a bond, with such surety or sureties as the proper officer
of customs may require, or both.


-----

## 6 Bill

6. _Clause 6 of the Bill seeks to delete section 30 of Act 438 as the authorisation_
for the conduct of prosecutions is to be dealt with under section 377 of the
Criminal Procedure Code.

7. _Clause 7 of the Bill seeks to introduce a new section 30A into Act 438 to_
impose a duty to keep the name and address of an informer and the substance
of the information received from an informer secret. A person who contravenes
this requirement shall be guilty of an offence and shall, on conviction, be liable
to a fine not exceeding ten thousand ringgit or to imprisonment for a term not
exceeding five years or to both.

8. _Clause 9 of the Bill seeks to amend section 33 of Act 438 to provide for_
the forfeiture of the amount secured under paragraph 21(1)(a) or 21(1)(b) of
Act 438 or of the amount realised by sale under paragraph 27(1)(c) of Act 438
when an offence is compounded.

9. Other amendments not specifically dealt with in this Statement are minor
or consequential in nature.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial expenditure.
