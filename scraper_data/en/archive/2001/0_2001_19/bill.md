EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Criminal Procedure Code ("Act 593").

_2. Clause 1 contains the short title and provisions on the commencement of the_
proposed Act.


-----

_Criminal Procedure Code (Amendment)_ _1_

3. Clause 2 seeks to replace the title of "Medical Superintendent" with "Medical
Director" in line with the creation of the post of Medical Director by the Mental
Health Act 2001, and to provide that the expression "Medical Director" will
include a "Deputy Medical Director". This amendment is consequential to the
enactment of the Mental Health Act 2001.

4. Clause 3 seeks to amend section 108A to delete the reference to section 108.

5. _Clauses 4,6, 7,5,9 and 10 are amendments consequential to the abolition of_
preliminary inquiries and trials by jury.

6. _Clauses 11,12,13,14,15,16,17,18,19and 21 are amendments consequential_
to the enactment of the Mental Health Act 2001.

_1._ _Paragraphs 20(a), (b),(c) and(d) contain amendments consequential to the_
proposed amendments to the Penal Code which will incorporate into it offences
currently existing in the Women and Girls Protection Act 1973 [Act 106] which
will be repealed when the Child Act 2001 [Act 611] comes into force. Paragraphs
_20(e) and (f) contain amendments consequential to the proposed amendments to_
the Penal Code which will create the offence of incest.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial expenditure the amount
of which cannot at present be ascertained.
