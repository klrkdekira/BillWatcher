EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Employees Provident Fund Act 1991 (''Act 452").

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and provisions on the commencement of_
the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 26B of Act 452. This amendment seeks_
to widen the meaning of "securities issued by the Government of Malaysia"
to include negotiable securities and instruments which are fully guaranteed by
the Government of Malaysia and Bank Negara Malaysia and negotiable securities
and instruments issued by a corporation which is fully owned by the Government
of Malaysia or Bank Negara Malaysia.


-----

_Employees Provident Fund (Amendment)                                                                           7_

4. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 44 of Act 452. With this amendment,_
the Board may receive any funds from any retirement scheme or plan relating
to an employee remitted by any other person or the employee's employer.

5. _Clause 5 seeks to amend section 54 of Act 452. Generally, this amendment_
seeks to enable a member of the Fund to withdraw a part of the amount
standing to his credit in the Fund to purchase or build a second house, to
finance his higher learning and to purchase a personal computer for himself.
This amendment also seeks to empower the Minister, if he thinks it expedient
and after consulting the Board, by order published in the Gazette, to add to,
vary or modify the purposes for which a member of the Fund may make a
withdrawal under subsection 54(6) of Act 452.

6. Clause 6 seeks to introduce a new section, namely section 54c, into Act
452. This amendment seeks to enable the Board to authorize the withdrawal
of all or part of the amount of money standing to the credit of the member
of the Fund who is a pensionable employee if the member has been required
to retire or had elected to retire under sections 10, 11, 12 and 12A of the
Pensions Act 1980 or sections 10, 11, 12 and 13 of the Statutory and Local
Authorities Pensions Act 1980.

7. Clause 8 seeks to amend Part VIA of Act 452 to correct the sequence of
provisions in the Act.

8. Other amendments not specifically mentioned are minor or consequential
in nature.

_FINANCIAL_ **_IMPLICATIONS_**

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial expenditure.
