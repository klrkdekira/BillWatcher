**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Promotion of Investments**
(Amendment) Act 2011.

and (b), and sections 21, 26, 27, 31, 35, 37, 42, 43, 46, 49 and 50
are deemed to have come into operation on 8 September 2007.

21 May 2003.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Promotion of Investments Act 1986 (“Act 327”).

2. _Clause 1_ contains the short title and date of commencement of the
proposed Act. The proposed Act is deemed to have come into operation on
8 September 2007 so as to implement the incentives introduced through the
2008 Budget.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 5 of Act 327—_

_(a) to introduce a new paragraph_ _(c) into subsection (1D) to enable a_
company establishing a medical devices testing laboratory to apply
in writing to the Minister for pioneer status;

_(b) to introduce a new proviso into subsection (1DB) to provide for the_
application for pioneer status by a company participating or intending
to participate in Malaysia in the generation of renewable energy other
than for its own consumption. This incentive is an enhancement to
the existing incentive and is now extended to related companies
within the same group;

_(c) to delete subsection (1DG) since the incentive for a company participating_
in information and communication technology or multimedia activities
including computer software development located outside cybercities
and cybercentres was discontinued in 2008 Budget; and

_(d) in subsection (2), to provide that all applications for pioneer status_
under section 5 shall be in accordance with the regulations made
under Act 327.

of Act 327 in consequence of the deletion of subsection 5(1DG) of Act 327.
