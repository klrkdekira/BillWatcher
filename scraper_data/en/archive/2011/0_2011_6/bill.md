An Act to amend the Malaysian Industrial Development Authority

[ ]

**ENACTED by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:**

**Short title**

**1. This Act may be cited as the Malaysian Industrial Development**
Authority (Incorporation) (Amendment) Act 2011.

**Amendment of long title**

**2. The Malaysian Industrial Development Authority (Incorporation)**
Act 1965 [Act 397], which is referred to as the “principal Act”
in this Act, is amended by substituting for the long title the
following long title:

“An Act to provide for the establishment and incorporation
of the Malaysian Investment Development Authority and to
provide for the administration, functions and powers of the
Authority and for matters connected therewith.”.

**Savings and transitional provisions**

**19. (1) All references to the Malaysian Industrial Development**
Authority (Incorporation) Act 1965 in any written law or document
shall, on the coming into operation of this Act, be construed as
references to the Malaysian Investment Development Authority
(Incorporation) Act 1965.

(2) The change of name of “The Malaysian Industrial Development
Authority” to “The Malaysian Investment Development Authority”
upon the coming into operation of this Act shall not affect any
rights or obligations of the Authority or render defective any
legal proceedings by or against it, and any legal proceedings that
could have been continued or commenced by or against it before
the date of coming into operation of this Act may be continued
or commenced under its new name.

(3) All actions, regulations, orders, directions, notifications,
approvals, decisions, guidelines and other executive acts made,
given or done under, or in accordance with, or by virtue of the
principal Act before the coming into operation of this Act shall be
deemed to have been made, given or done under, or in accordance
with, or by virtue of, the corresponding provision introduced or
amended by this Act, and shall continue to remain in full force
and effect in relation to the persons to whom they apply until
amended, repealed or rescinded under, in accordance with, or by
virtue of, the corresponding provision introduced or amended by
this Act.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Malaysian Industrial Development Authority
(Incorporation) Act 1965 (“Act 397”).

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title of the proposed Act._

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend the long title of Act 397 to reflect the change of_
the name of the Malaysian Industrial Development Authority to the Malaysian
Investment Development Authority.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 1 of Act 397 to amend the short title_
of Act 397. The Act is to be known after this as the “Malaysian Investment
Development Authority (Incorporation) Act 1965”.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 397 to introduce new definitions_
and to amend existing definitions used in Act 397.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend section 3 of Act 397 to provide for the re-_
establishment of the Malaysian Industrial Development Authority as the
Malaysian Investment Development Authority.

7. _Clause 7 seeks to amend section 3D in consequence of the change of title_
of the Director General to the Chief Executive Officer. This clause also seeks
to empower the Chief Executive Officer to manage the annual budget of the
Authority.


-----

_Malaysian Industrial Development Authority_ 11
_(Incorporation) (Amendment)_

8. _Clause 8 seeks to amend section 6 of Act 397 to list out the new extended_
functions of the Authority.

9. _Clause 9 seeks to amend section 6A of Act 397 to provide for the new_
powers of the Authority. The Authority now has the power to require Government
Entities, companies and other bodies or persons responsible for or carrying
out or intending to carry out development in Malaysia to submit reports to
the Authority with regard to their activities.

10. _Clause 12 seeks to introduce a new section 7C into Act 397 to empower_
the Authority to establish companies.

11. _Clause 14 seeks to amend section 10A of Act 397 to allow the Fund_
to be expended for the giving of loans or any financial assistance to the
private sectors and for the giving of financial assistance, credit facility or
contribution to any person for any educational purposes, scholarships and any
other purposes.

12. _Clause 15 seeks to introduce a new section 10AA into Act 397 to allow_
the Authority to invest moneys of the Authority.

13. _Clause 16 seeks to amend section 12 of Act 397 to allow the Authority_
to furnish its Annual Report to the Minister at a more practicable time.

14. _Clause 17 seeks to amend section 13 of Act 397 to provide an increase_
in penalty for the offence of breaching the duty to maintain secrecy.

15. _Clause 19 provides for savings and transitional provisions._

16. Other amendments not specifically dealt with in this Statement are minor
or consequential in nature.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial expenditure the amount
