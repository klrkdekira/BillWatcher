A BILL

_i n t i t u l e d_

An Act to amend the Lembaga Pembangunan Industri Pembinaan
Malaysia Act 1994.

[ ]

**ENACTED** by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:

**Short title and commencement**

**1.** (1) This Act may be cited as the Lembaga Pembangunan
Industri Pembinaan Malaysia (Amendment) Act 2011.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed

by the Minister by notification in the _Gazette and the Minister_
may appoint different dates for the coming into operation of
different provisions of this Act.

This Bill seeks to amend the Lembaga Pembangunan Industri Pembinaan
Malaysia Act 1994 (“Act 520”).

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and provisions on the commencement of_
the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to substitute the existing section 2 of Act 520, with some_
new definitions inserted, among others—

_(a) in relation to the definition of “contractor”, to allow any person who_

merely undertakes to carry out and complete construction works to be
regarded as “contractor” also and thus is required to be registered;

_(b) in relation to the definition of “construction industry”, to include_

design, technology, material and workmanship, taking into account
the current trend of the industry;

_(c) in relation to the definition of “construction works”, to include the_

procurement of construction materials, equipment or workers, as
necessarily required for the actualization of any nature of construction
works as defined in the existing Act 520;

_(d) to provide for the definition of “construction personnel” to include_

general construction workers, semi-skilled construction workers,
skilled construction workers, construction site supervisors, construction
project managers and any employee or class of employees as may
be determined by the Lembaga, and includes any person who is
involved or undertakes to be involved in any activities relating to
construction works;

_(e) to provide for the definition of “construction project manager”, to_

allow any person to be recognized and registered as “construction
project manager” at the discretion of the Lembaga if he fulfills
the qualification, skill, experience and knowledge required by the
Lembaga;

_(f) to provide for the definition of “deal” to mean manufacturing, using,_

supplying, marketing, selling or buying whether wholesale or retail,
importing or exporting in order to control quality and conformance
of standards at source;

IBS”; and
