EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Lembaga Letrik Sabah Act 1983 (the
"Act").


-----

## 4 Bill

_2. Clause 2 seeks to amend section 3 of the Act by inserting a_
new subsection (1A) which empowers the Lembaga Letrik Sabah
(the "Board"), with the approval of the Minister, to privatise or
transfer or dispose of all or any of its activities, assets and liabilities.

3. Clause 3 seeks to introduce new Part X which contains provisions
for the dissolution of the Board and the effect of such dissolution.

Section 91 provides for the power of the Minister to appoint a

date of dissolution of the Board when he is satisfied that by virtue
of the privatisation or transfer or disposal of all or more than half
of the activities of the Board or of all or more than half its assets,
the Board should not continue to exist.

Section 92 provides that on the dale of dissolution, the Act shall

be repealed and the Board shall stand dissolved.

Section 93 provides for the devolution of rights, privileges,

liabilities and obligations of the Board to the Government.

Section 94 provides for the transfer of all movable and immovable

property and other assets of the Board to the Government or the
Federal Lands Commissioner, as the case may be, and for the transfer
of all moneys standing in, or due to be paid, to the Board, to the
Government.

Section 95 provides for the enforcement of all existing contracts

by or against the Government in substitution of the Board.

Section 96 provides for continuance of legal proceedings by or

against the Government in substitution of the Board.

Section 97 provides for the continuance of all written laws which

affect or protect the Board with references to the Board being construed
as references to the Government.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure.
