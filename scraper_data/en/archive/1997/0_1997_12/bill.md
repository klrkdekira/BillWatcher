EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Promotion of Investments Act 1986
("the Act").

2. Clause 1 deals with preliminary matters.

3. Clause 2 seeks to introduce a new definition into the Act and
to amend the definition of certain terms in the Act.

4. Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection 5(lA) of the Act to allow
any company or person, which intends to establish or participate
in an activity or participate in the production of a product which
is of national and strategic importance to Malaysia, to apply for
pioneer status. A new proviso to subsection 5(lA) of the Act is
introduced to allow a company, which is already operating in Malaysia
and is participating in an activity or in the production of a product
which is of national and strategic importance to Malaysia, to apply
for pioneer status if that company fulfills such conditions as may
be determined by the Minister. This clause also seeks to amend
paragraph 5(lD)(a) of the Act to allow a company which is
participating in a promoted activity or producing a promoted product
in an industrial linkage programme to apply for pioneer status.

5. _Clause 5 seeks to amend subsection 21 c(2) of the Act to introduce_

a proviso to paragraph (b) of that subsection which provides for
the computation of the income of a company that has been operating
in Malaysia for each accounting period of its pioneer business. A
new subsection, namely subsection (2A), is introduced into section
21c of the Act to provide for the definition of the term "value-
added income" and the determination of "inflation-adjusted base
income".

6. Clause 6 seeks to amend subsection 21B(l) of the Act to provide
for the computation of the income of a pioneer company participating
in a promoted activity or producing a promoted product in an industrial
linkage programme.

7. _Clause 7 seeks to amend subsection 26A(1) of the Act to introduce_

a proviso to that subsection to allow a company, that has been
operating in Malaysia and is participating in a promoted activity
or producing a promoted product, to apply for approval for the
purposes of an investment tax allowance if it fulfills such conditions
as may be determined by the Minister.

8. Clause 8 seeks to amend subsection 26F(l) of the Act to allow
a company participating in a promoted activity or producing a

promoted product in an industrial linkage programme to apply for
approval for purposes of an investment tax allowance.

9. Clause 10 seeks to amend section 29B of the Act to introduce
a new subsection, namely subsection (2A), which provides for the
computation of the investment tax allowance of a company that has
been operating in Malaysia.

10. Clause 11 seeks to amend section 29G of the Act to provide

for the computation of the investment tax allowance of a company
participating in a promoted activity or producing a promoted product
in an industrial linkage programme.

11. Clause 12 seeks to amend subsection 4lB(l) of the Act by

deleting paragraph (a) of the proviso thereto to give a wider coverage
to the term "capital expenditure" for the purpose of determining the
infrastructure allowance to be given to a company.

12. Other amendments not specifically referred to are minor or

consequential in nature.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure. [PN. (U[2]) 1996.]
