A BILL

# intituled

An Act to amend the Judges' Remuneration Act 1971.

[ ]

BE IT ENACTED by the Seri Paduka Baginda Yang

di-Pertuan Agong with the advice and consent of the
Dewan Negara and Dewan Rakyat in Parliament
assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:

**1. This Act may be cited as the Judges' Remuneration**
(Amendment) Act 1997 and shall be deemed to have

come into force on 1 January 1995.

**2. The Judges' Remuneration Act 1971 is amended in**
section 7 by substituting for the words "1/20 of his last
drawn salary for each completed month of his service"
the words "seven and a half per centum of the amount
## arrived at by multiplying his total completed months of
service as a Judge by his last drawn salary".

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend section 7 of the Judges' Remuneration
Act 1971 so that "Pekeliling Perkhidmatan Bilangan 2 Tahun 1996"
is also made applicable to judges.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial expenditure
the amount of which cannot at present be ascertained.
