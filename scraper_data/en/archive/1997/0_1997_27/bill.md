A BILL

# intituled

An Act to amend the Central Bank of Malaysia Act
## 1958.

[ ]

BE IT ENACTED by the Seri Paduka Baginda Yang
di-Pertuan Agong with the advice and consent of the
Dewan Negara and Dewan Rakyat in Parliament
assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:

**1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Central Bank of**
## Malaysia (Amendment) Act 1997.

(2) This Act shall come into force on a date to be
appointed by the Minister by notification in the Gazette.

**2. The Central Bank of Malaysia Act 1958, which in**
this Act is referred to as the "principal Act", is amended
in section 16A by inserting after the words "Banking
and Financial Institutions Act 1989," the words "the
## Money-Changing Act 1997,".

**3. Subsection 30(3) of the principal Act is amended—**

_(a) by substituting for the word "and" appearing_
after the words "Takaful Act 1984" a comma;
and

_(b) by inserting after the words "the Banking and_
Financial Institutions Act 1989" the words "and
the Money-Changing Act 1997,".

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Central Bank of Malaysia Act 1958.

2. Clause 1 deals with the short title and commencement of the
proposed Act.

3. Clauses 2, 3, 4 and 5 seek to amend sections 16A, 30, 34 and
37 which are amendments consequential to the enactment of a separate
Act on money-changing.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure. [PN. (U[2]) 2021.]
