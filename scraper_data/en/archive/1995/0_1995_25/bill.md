A BILL

# intituled

An Act to provide for the vesting of property, rights and
liabilities of the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar in
a company, to make financial arrangements for that
company, to provide for matters relating to staff and
for other matters connected therewith.

**3. (1) The Minister may, by order published in the**
_Gazette, appoint a vesting date and on that date, all_
property, rights or liabilities of the Perbadanan
Pembangunan Bandar shall by virtue of this Act be
transferred to and vested in the successor company without
any conveyance, assignment or transfer whatever.

(2) Every property vested by virtue of subsection (1)
in the successor company shall be so vested in the company
for the like title or interest as the same was vested or
held immediately before the vesting date.

(3) Every chose-in-action vested by virtue of subsection
### (1) in the successor company may, after the vesting date,
be sued on, recovered or enforced by the succcessor
company in its own name and it shall not be necessary
for the successor company or the Perbadanan Pembangunan
Bandar to give notice to the person bound by the chose### in-action of the vesting effected by subsection (1).

(4) Every right and liability vested by subsection (1)
in the successor company may, on and after the vesting
date, be sued on, recovered or enforced by or against
the successor company in its own name and it shall not
be necessary for the successor company or the Perbadanan
Pembangunan Bandar to give notice to the person whose
right or liability is affected by the vesting under subsection
(1).

(5) Any pending legal proceedings by or against the
Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar which relate to any
property, right or liability transferred to and vested in
### the successor company by virtue of subsection (1) may,
on and after the vesting date, be continued by or against
the successor company.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to provide for the vesting of property, rights and
liabilities of the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar to a successor
company, to make financial arrangement for that company and to
provide for matters relating to staff.

2. Clause 1 deals with the short title and commencement date.

3. _Clause 2 defines certain words and expressions used in the_
proposed Act.

4. Clause 3 seeks to provide that on a vesting date to be appointed
by the Minister by order published in the Gazette, all property,
rights and liabilities of the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar that
are specified in the Order, are to be transferred to and vested in
the successor company as if they were the property, rights and
liabilities of the successor company.

_Subclause 3(8) seeks to provide that the transfer to and vesting_
of rights in the successor company does not include the right of
the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar to regulate matters under section
18 of the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar Act 1971.

5. Clause 4 seeks to provide for the securities of the successor
company, to be issued or alloted to the Corporation as the Minister
of Finance, may direct, from time to time after consultation with
the Minister.

_Subclause 4(2) provides for the issuance of securities at nominal_
values to be determined by the Minister of Finance and issued as
having been fully paid and deemed to have been paid at nominal
value to the successor company.

_Subclause 4(3) empowers the Minister of Finance after consulting_
the Minister to dispose of any securities already issued or any rights
to securities initially allotted to the Corporation.

_Subclause 4(4) seeks to provide that dividend acquired by the_
Corporation through the securities and the rights shall be paid into
the Consolidated Fund.

6. _Suclauses 5(1) and (2) seek to empower the Corporation after_
consulting the Minister, at any time to acquire the securities of the
successor company or its subsidiaries or to subscribe for securities,
and dispose of any such securities acquired.

_Subclause 5(3) seeks to provide that all expenses incurred by the_
Corporation shall be deemed as investment allowed under section
_8(3)(a)(iv) of the Financial Procedure Act 1957._

_Subclause 5(4) seeks to provide that the dividend or any payment_
received by the Corporation for the disposal of securities shall be
paid into the Consolidated Fund.

_Subclause 5(5) seeks to provide that stamp duty for any increase_
in the capital of the successor company is not chargeable if the
successor company is still wholly owned by the Government and
the Treasury certifies that the shares of the successor company is
wholly subscribed by the Minister of Finance.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to provide that the Minister of Finance after_
consultation with the Minister may appoint nominees to act on his
behalf for purposes of clauses 4 and 5.

8. Subclauses 7(1) and (2) seek to provide that if at any time
before the successor company ceases to be wholly owned by the
Government, the Minister of Finance after consultation with the
Minister, so directs, a sum not exceeding the accumulated realised
profits of the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar shall be paid into
the statutory reserve for the purpose of payment of fully paid bonus
share.

_Subclauses 7(3) and (4) seek to provide that statutory account_
for the successor company shall be based on the statement of account
of the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar for the year ending before
the vesting date and other related matter shall be adopted by the
successor company.

_Subclause 7(6) provides that the statutory account of the successor_

company for purposes of any provisions of the Companies Act 1965.

_Subclause 7(7) provides that a complete accounting year means_
an accounting year ending 31 December or a period to be determined
by the Minister of Finance.

9. Clause 8 seeks to provide for matters relating to employees of
the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar. Every such employee who
was given an option by the Perbadanan Pembangunan Bandar and
who opts to serve as an employee of the successor company shall
be employed by the successor company on terms and conditions of
service not less favourable than those to which the employee was
entitled to immediately before the appointed date.
