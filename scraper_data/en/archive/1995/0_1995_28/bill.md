EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Strata Titles Act 1985.

2. Clause 1 deals with the short title of the Bill.

3. Clause 2 seeks to insert the definition of "special resolution" and to
amend the definition of "unanimous resolution" in section 4 in order to
facilitate the management of the subdivided building.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection 6(1) to allow a building having_
only one storey in the same strata scheme to be subdivided into parcels to
be held under strata titles or into accessory parcels.

5. Clause 4 seeks to amend section 7 by inserting two new subsections,
namely subsections (2) and (3), to allow the proprietor of abuilding on any
alienated land which has been duly surveyed held under qualified title or
where the certificate of fitness for occupation has yet to be issued to submit
an application to the Land Office for subdivision thereof into parcels.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend subsection 9(1) by inserting the new_
paragraphs (k) and (l) to provide for the conditions for the approval of an
application for subdivision of any building on a land held under qualified
title or where the certificate of fitness for occupation has not been issued.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend subparagraph I0(1)(b)(i) to include any_
approved amendments to the original building plans as part of the building
plans. The new paragraph (cb) seeks to require the proprietor of a land held
under qualified title to submit three copies of the certified plan of the land
for the application for subdivision of the building. Subsection 10(8) has
been amended to outline the necessary actions to be taken by a Land
Administrator on receiving an application for subdivision of building. It
also seeks to introduce a further proviso to subsection 10(10) to outline the
subsequent actions to be carried out by the Land Administrator in respect
of any building on a land held under qualified title or where the certificate
of fitness for occupation has not been issued.

8. Clause 7 seeks to amend subsection 10A(1) to introduce a proviso to
the effect that a building having only one storey shall not be included in an
application for a provisional strata title or titles for the provisional block
or blocks.

9. Clause 8 seeks to amend subsection 13(2) by inserting a new paragraph
_(c) to require that a certified strata plan shall contain a schedule showing_
the approved share units of each parcel and the total share units of all the
parcels in the certified strata plan approved by the Director of Survey.
_Clause 8 further seeks to introduce a new subsection (5) to provide for the_
matters to be contained in a certified plan in respect of a provisional block.

10. Clause 9 seeks to amend Form 1 in the First Schedule by inserting
subparagraph (ea) which is necessitated by the introduction of paragraph
10(l)(cb).

11. Clause 10 seeks to delete paragraph 18 in the Second Schedule as the
definition of "special resolution" is now proposed to be introduced in
section 4 of the Act.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial
expenditure. [PN. (U[2]) 1866.]
