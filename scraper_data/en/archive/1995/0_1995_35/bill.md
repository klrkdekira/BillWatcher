PART I

PRELIMINARY

**1. This Act may be cited as the Labuan Offshore Financial**
Services Authority Act 1995 and shall come into force
on such date as the Minister may, by notification in the
_Gazette, appoint; and the Minister may appoint different_
dates for the coming into force of different provisions
of this Act.

"accounting officer" includes every officer and servant

accounts for, any money of the Fund, or who is charged
with the receipt, custody or disposal of, or the accounting
for, any store and property of the Authority or who in

THE AUTHORITY

**3. (1) There is hereby established a body corporate by**
the name of "Labuan Offshore Financial Services
Authority" with perpetual succession and a common seal,
which may sue and be sued in its name and, subject to
and for the purposes of this Act, may enter into contracts
and may acquire, purchase, take, hold and enjoy movable

interest therein vested in the Authority upon such terms
as it deems fit.

(2) The objectives of the Authority are—

_(a) to promote and develop Labuan as a centre for_
offshore financial services;

_(b) to develop national objectives, policies and_
priorities for the orderly development and
administration of offshore financial services in
Labuan, and to make recommendations to the
#### Minister in respect thereof.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to create a body corporate to be known as the
Labuan Offshore Financial Services Authority (hereinafter referred
to as the "Authority") which will be charged with the responsibility
for licensing, supervising and regulating offshore financial services
and activities in Labuan.

PART I

2. Part I relates to preliminary matters.


-----

LABUAN OFFSHORE FINANCIAL SERVICES 25

AUTHORITY

_Clause 1 seeks to provide for the short title and the commencement_
of the proposed Act. Under this clause the Minister is empowered
to appoint different dates for the coming into force of different
provisions of the Bill.

_Clause 2 defines certain words and expressions used in the Bill,_
including "Authority", "Fund", "offshore financial institution" and
"offshore financial services".

PART II

3. Part II contains provisions relating to the Authority.

_Clause 3 provides for the establishment of the Authority in the_
form of a body corporate to be known as the "Labuan Offshore
Financial Services Authority", and sets out the objectives of the
Authority.

_Clause 4 deals with the functions of the Authority, which include_
administering and enforcing the offshore financial services legislation
and co-operating with offshore financial institutions in Labuan to
promote offshore financial services.

_Clause 5 seeks to provide for the composition of the Authority_
whose members shall be appointed by the Minister. The members
of the Authority, including the Chairman and the Director-General,
shall not exceed nine. This clause also provides that the DirectorGeneral shall be the chief executive officer of the Authority.

_Clause 6 deals with the tenure of office of the members of the_
Authority.

_Clause 7 deals with the resignation of members of the Authority_
and revocation of their appointment as members.

_Clause 8 deals with vacation of office of members of the Authority._

_Clause 9 seeks to enable the Minister to give general directions_
to the Authority but such directions must be consistent with the
provisions of the Act.

_Clause 10 seeks to enable the Authority to determine its own_
procedure but subject to the approval of the Minister.

_Clause 11 enables the Authority to invite other persons to attend_
meetings of the Authority. The invitee however will not have the
right to vote at such meetings.


-----

_Clause 12 provides for the remuneration and allowance of members_
of the Authority and persons invited to attend meetings of the
Authority.

_Clause 13 provides for the Authority to have a common seal_
which shall be taken judicial notice of for all official purposes.

_Clause 14 seeks to enable the Authority to delegate any of its_
functions or powers.

_Clause 15 allows the Authority to establish committees to assist_
the Authority in performing its functions.

_Clause 16 deals with the minutes of meetings of the Authority_
and committees which shall be kept and maintained in proper form.

_Clause 17 requires a member of the Authority or committee to_
disclose his share or interest in, inter alia, any contract made with
the Authority, any beneficial interest in land proposed to be acquired
or otherwise dealt with by the Authority and any share or interest
in any offshore financial institution.

_Clause 18 provides for the employment of officers and servants_
of the Authority.

_Clause 19 deals with the power of the Authority to grant loans_
and make advances to its officers and servants.

_Clause 20 seeks to confer on the Authority disciplinary control_
over its officers and servants and for this purpose the Authority
may set up disciplinary committees.

_Clause 21 deals with the question of appeal against the decision_
of a disciplinary committee.

_Clause 22 deals with the power of the Authority to make_
disciplinary regulations, subject to the approval of the Minister,
and such regulations may create disciplinary offences and provide
for disciplinary punishments.

_Clause 23 seeks to allow the Authority to impose surcharge in_
respect of its officers and servants and sets out when the Authority
may do so.

_Clause 24 requires the Authority to inform the person to be_
surcharged.

_Clause 25 deals with withdrawal of surcharge._
