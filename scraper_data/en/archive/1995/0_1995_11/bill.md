EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to create a body corporate to be known as the
"Perbadanan Putrajaya" which will be charged with the responsibility
of promoting and stimulating the development of Putrajaya, particularly
as a Federal Government administrative centre.

2. Part I contains preliminary matters.

_Clause 1 contains the short title and provisions on the_
commencement of the proposed Act.

_Clause 2 contains the definitions of several terms used in this_
Bill.

3. Part II contains provisions relating to the Perbadanan Putrajaya.

_Clause 3 provides for the establishment and objective of the_
Perbadanan, while clause 4 deals with its functions and powers which
include powers to privatise its activities.

_Clause 5 contains provisions on the membership of the Perbadanan._

_Clause 9 seeks to empower the Minister to give directions to_
the Perbadanan.

_Clause 10 contains a description of the area within which the_
Perbadanan will perform its functions.


-----

PERBADANAN PUTRAJAYA 25

_Clause 11 contains provisions which enable the Federal or State_
Government to assign to the Perbadanan other additional functions
to be performed by the Perbadanan.

_Clause 12 requires the Perbadanan to furnish returns, reports,_
accounts and information to the Minister and such other public
authority as directed by the Minister.

4. Part III of the Bill contains provisions on the President, Chief
Executive and officers and servants of the Perbadanan.

_Clause 13 deals with the duties of the President of the Perbadanan._

_Clause 14 contains provisions in respect of the chief executive_
officer of the Perbadanan.

_Clause 15 seeks to empower the Perbadanan to appoint its officers_
and servants.

_Clauses 16 and 17 seek to empower the Perbadanan, with the_
approval of the Minister, to make regulations on the conditions of
service and the discipline of its officers and servants.

5. Part IV contains financial provisions.

_Clause 20 provides for the establishment of a Fund and clause_
_21 seeks to impose upon the Perbadanan the duty of conserving such_
Fund.

_Clause 22 specifies the expenditure which may be charged on_
the Fund.

6. Part V deals with the other powers and functions of the Perbadanan.

_Clause 25 allows the Perbadanan to delegate certain functions,_
powers and duties to the Chief Executive.

_Clause 26 provides for the borrowing of money by the Perbadanan_
and clause 27 seeks to enable the Perbadanan to invest its assets.

The Perbadanan is also allowed to establish corporations under
_clause 29._

7. Part VI contains general provisions.

_Clause 31 seeks to exempt the Perbadanan from the payment of_
stamp duties.

_Clause 32 prohibits a member, officer or servant of the Perbadanan_
from disclosing information obtained in the course of his duties.


-----

26 BILL

_Clause 33 deals with the power of the Perbadanan to make_
regulations.
