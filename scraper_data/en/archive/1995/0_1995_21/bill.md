PART I

2. Part I contains preliminary matters.

_Clause 1 deals with the short title and commencement of the_

Act. Clause 2 defines certain words and expressions used in the
Act.

PART II

3. Part II contains provisions relating to the Board of Town Planners
("the Board").

_Clause 3 establishes the Board. Clauses 4 to 7 provide for matters_

relating to the membership of the Board, termination and revocation
of appointment and filling of vacancy. Clause 8 spells out the functions
of the Board. Clause 9 empowers the Board to borrow money. The
Board is allowed to appoint committees to assist it in clause 10.

PART III

4. Part III contains provisions pertaining to the registration of
Town Planners.

_Clause 11 deals with the keeping and maintenance of a Register_

of registered Town Planners and registered Graduate Town Planners.
_Clause 12 provides for the appointment of a Registrar of Town_
Planners and his duties. Clause 13 sets out the entitlement of a
registered Town Planner. Restrictions on unregistered Town Planners
and registered Graduate Town Planners are spelt out in clause 14.
_Clause 15 prescribes the qualification for registration as a registered_
Graduate Town Planner and registered Town Planner. Clauses 16
and 17 provide for the manner of application for registration and
the form of the certificate of registration. Clause 18 deals with the
temporary registration of foreign Town Planners. A duty is imposed
on a registered Town Planner, registered Graduate Town Planner
and foreign Town Planner to notify the Registrar of any change of
address in clause 19.

Part IV

5. Part IV contains provisions dealing with town planning practice
by bodies corporate.

_Clause 20 describes the composition of the board of directors_

and shareholders of a body corporate which may practice as a Town
Planner. Clause 21 among other things, allows a body corporate
practising as a Town Planner to use the title "registered Town Planner".
_Clause 22 deals with the manner of application for permit. Clause_
_23 provides for the form of the permit. Under clause 24 the body_
corporate is to notify the Board of any change in the composition
of its board of directors or shareholders and change of its business
address. The Board may cancel a permit under clause 25 but only
after a hearing of the grounds of complaint has been conducted and
right to be heard has been accorded as required under clause 26.
_Clause 27 requires a body corporate to cease its practice as a Town_

Planner once the Board cancels its permit. Clause 28 pertains to
the surrender of the permit upon its cancellation. The Board is
empowered to issue fresh permits under clause 29. Matters under

PART V

6. Part V contains provisions pertaining to the cancellation of
registration, suspension from practice, removal from and reinstatement
into the Register.

_Clause 31 provides for the appointment of an Investigation_
Committee and a Disciplinary Committee. Clause 32 relates to
suspension of registration pending investigation. Clause 33 provides
for orders that may be made by the Disciplinary Committee under
the circumstances stated in clause 34. Clause 35 spells out the
circumstances under which the registration of a registered Town
Planner or registered Graduate Town Planner is null and void. Matters
pertaining to the removal from the Register and the return of the
certificate of registration to the Board upon removal are contained
in clauses 36 and 37. Clause 38 on the other hand provides for

_Clause 44 provides for certain offences under the Act and its_
