**Construction of reference to the principal Act**

**4. Where, on and after the commencement date, a reference is**
made in any written law to the "Institut Teknologi MARA Act
1976", such reference shall be construed as a reference to the
"Universiti Teknologi MARA Act 1976".

**Change of name of Institut**

**5.** (1) The Institut Teknologi MARA established under section 3
###### of the principal Act shall, on and after the commencement date,
be known as the "Universiti Teknologi MARA".

(2) All references to the Institut Teknologi MARA in any written
law or in any instrument, deed, title, document, bond, agreement
or working arrangement subsisting immediately before the
commencement date shall, on and after the commencement date,
be construed as references to the Universiti Teknologi MARA.

(3) The change of name from the "Institut Teknologi MARA"
to the "Universiti Teknologi MARA" shall not affect any powers,
rights, privileges, liabilities, duties or obligations of the Institut
Teknologi MARA, or render defective any legal proceedings by
or against it.

(4) Legal proceedings that could have been continued or
commenced by or against the Institut Teknologi MARA prior to
the commencement date may be continued or commenced after
the commencement date in the name of the Universiti Teknologi
MARA.

**6. All existing liabilities incurred by or on behalf of or for the**
purposes of the Institut Teknologi MARA may, on and after the
commencement date, be enforced against the Universiti Teknologi
MARA.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Institut Teknologi MARA Act 1976 ("Act 173").

_3._ _Clause 2 seeks to define the term "commencement date" to mean the date of_
coming into operation of the proposed Act.

_4._ _Clause 3 seeks to make general amendments to Act 173 by replacing the_
words "INSTITUT" and "Institut" with the words "UNIVERSITI" and
"Universiti" respectively. With this amendment, Act 173 will now be entitled the
"Universiti Teknologi MARA Act 1976". Clause 4 seeks to specify that all
references in any written law to the old title will be construed as references to the
new title.

_5._ _Clause 5 seeks to give effect to the change of name of the Institut. The Institut_
will now be known as the "Universiti Teknologi MARA". It also provides that the
powers, rights, privileges, liabilities, duties and obligations of the Institut, and
legal proceedings by or against the Institut, will not be affected by the change of
name of the Institut.

_6._ _Clause 6 seeks to provide that all existing liabilities incurred by the Institut_
may be enforced against the Universiti.

_7._ _Clause 7 deals with the vesting of the lands, property and assets of the Institut_
in the Universiti.

_8._ _Clause 8 seeks to provide for the transfer of moneys and property from the_
Institut Teknologi MARA Fund to the Universiti Teknologi MARA Fund. A
similar transfer is also provided for with regard to moneys contained in the reserve
fund or any other fund of the Institut.

_9._ _Clause 9 provides that the Branches, campuses, Schools and Centres of the_
Institut shall, on the commencement date of the proposed Act, be deemed to be the
Branches, campuses, Schools and Centres of the Universiti.

_10._ _Clause 10 seeks to provide that on the commencement date of the proposed_
Act, the Board of Directors and the Senate of the Institut shall be deemed to be the
Board of Directors and the Senate of the Universiti.

_11._ _Clause 11 seeks to ensure the continuity of service of the staff, agents and_
other persons appointed or employed by the Institut with the Universiti from the
date the proposed Act comes into operation.

12. By clause 12, all students of the Institut are deemed to be the students of the
Universiti following the same courses of study they were following prior to the
commencement date of the proposed Act.

_13._ _Clause 13 deals with the changeover from Rector to Vice-Chancellor._
References to the Rector of the Institut will now be construed as references to the
Vice-Chancellor of the Universiti.
