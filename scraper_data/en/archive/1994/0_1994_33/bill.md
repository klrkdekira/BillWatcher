EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Income Tax Act 1967, the Petroleum
(Income Tax) Act 1967, the Stamp Act 1949 and the Promotion of
Investments Act 1986.

2. Clause 4 seeks to introduce a new section 3c to exempt resident

Malaysia and remitted to Malaysia. This exemption shall not apply to
resident companies carrying on the business of banking, insurance,

year of assessment 1995.
