EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Employees Provident Fund Act 1991
("the Act").

2. Clause 1 seeks to provide for preliminary matters and the date
for the coming into force of the Bill.

3. Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act to introduce
certain new definitions for words used in the Act.

4. Clause 3 seeks to amend section 4 of the Act by deleting the
proviso to subsection (4).

5. Clause 4 seeks to amend section 13 of the Act for purposes of
authentication of the Board's common seal.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to introduce a new paragraph 24(2)(b) of the Act_

to enable moneys belonging to the Fund to be expended for the
purpose of investment under this Act. It also seeks to amend
paragraph 24(2)(d) to enable moneys belonging to the Fund to be
expended for payment for services of any persons employed or

appointed by the Board. This clause further allows for other
expenses, cost or expenditure properly incurred or accepted by the
Board in the execution of its powers and the carrying out of its
duties.


-----

20 BILL

7. Clause 6 seeks to introduce a new section 26 to define the
investment powers of the Board and the form of investments
allowed.

8. Clause 7 seeks to introduce two new sections 26A and 26e
giving power to the Board to invest in approved companies and in
securities issued by the Government of Malaysia respectively.

9. Clause 8 seeks to amend section 27 of the Act requiring the
Board to seek the approval of the Minister in declaring dividends.

10. _Clause 9 seeks to amend subsection 30(2) of the Act to provide_
