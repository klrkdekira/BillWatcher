EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Tabung Angkatan Tentera Act 1973
("the Act").

2. Clause 1 deals with the commencement of this Bill.

3. Clause 2 seeks to make general amendments to the Act. With
these amendments, the Act will now be entitled Tabung Angkatan
Tentera and Tabung Bekas Perajurit Act 1973.

4. Clause 3 seeks to correct errors in the national language text of
the Act.

5. Clause 4 clearly provides that the change of name of the Act
will not affect the reference to the Tabung Angkatan Tentera in any
written law or in any instrument or document relating to it nor will it

affect the rights, privileges, liabilities, duties or obligations of the
Lembaga.


-----

6 BILL

6. Clause 5 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act to introduce new
definitions for purposes of clarity in relation to the proposed
establishment of the Tabung Bekas Perajurit.

7. Clause 7 seeks to introduce new section 3A into the Act to
provide for the establishment of the Tabung Bekas Perajurit. The
objectives of this proposed new Tabung is to promote the socioeconomic development and welfare of, and to provide other benefits
for, the retiring personnel or retired personnel of the regular forces
of Malaysia.

8. Clause 8 seeks to amend section 4 of the Act. The amendment to
subsection (1) is necessitated by the introduction of the new section

**3A.**

9. Clause 9 seeks to amend section 5 of the Act consequent upon
the proposed amendment made to subsection 4(1) by clause 8.

10. Clause 10 seeks to amend subsections 6(1) and 15(1) of the Act
to provide for matters pertaining to the investment of the assets of
the Tabung Bekas Perajurit.

11. Clause 11 seeks to amend section 22 of the Act. New
paragraphs (aa) and (ga) seek to empower the Lembaga to specify
the terms and conditions to be met in order for any retiring personnel
or retired personnel to qualify for any benefits under the Act and to
set the terms and conditions for granting any loan out of the Tabung
Bekas Perajurit.

12. Other proposed amendments to the Act not specifically
referred to are consequential or minor in nature.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial expenditure
the amount of which cannot at present be ascertained.
