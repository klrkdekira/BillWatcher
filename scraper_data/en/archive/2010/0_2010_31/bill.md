“Committees of the Board

**11A.** (1) The Board may establish any committee as it
considers necessary or expedient to assist it in the performance
of its objects and powers under this Act.

(2) The Board may elect a member of the Board to be
the chairman of a committee.

(3) The Board may appoint any person to be a member
of a committee established under subsection (1).

(4) A member of a committee shall hold office for such
term as may be specified in his letter of appointment and is
eligible for reappointment.

(5) The Board may revoke the appointment of a member of a
committee without assigning any reason for the revocation.

(6) A member of a committee may, at any time, resign
by giving a one month notice in writing to the Chairman of
the Board.

(7) The Board may, at any time, discontinue or alter the
constitution of a committee.

(8) A committee may regulate its own procedure.”.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Bank Simpanan Nasional Act 1974 (“Act 146”).

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and commencement of the proposed_
Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 146 to provide for a change_
of the title of General Manager to Chief Executive.

4. Clause 3 seeks to amend section 5 of Act 146 to empower the Bank to
provide, operate and manage any other products or services as may be necessary
for the purpose of attaining the objects of the Bank and impose charges for
such products and services.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 6 of Act 146. The amendment seeks to_
remove the representatives of Bank Negara Malaysia and the Postal Services
Department from the membership of the Board. The amendment also seeks to
in crease the number of persons of standing and experience in matters relating
to banking, business or industry in membership of the Board of Directors from
not more than four persons to not less than four persons.

6. _Clause 6 seeks to amend section 9 of Act 146 in consequence of the_
change of the title of General Manager to Chief Executive.

7. _Clause 7 seeks to introduce a new section 11A into Act 146 to provide_
for the establishment of committees to assist the Board.

8. _Clause 9 seeks to introduce new sections 14A, 14B, 14C and 14D into_
Act 146 to provide for disciplinary matters relating to the officers and
employees of the Bank. The proposed section 14A provides that the Bank
shall have disciplinary authority and control over its officers and employees
and is empowered to make disciplinary regulations. The establishment of
disciplinary committees for the different categories of officers and employees

establishment of the Disciplinary Appeal Board. The proposed section 14D

consequential in nature.
