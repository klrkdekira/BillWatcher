“Authorized left turns

**76A. (1) Notwithstanding a red signal or other traffic sign**
indicating a place where traffic must stop at any traffic
junction, any person driving a motor vehicle on a road may
turn left at the traffic junction in accordance with any traffic
sign authorizing such left turn that has been lawfully sited
on such road after stopping before the marked stop line on
the road to ascertain that it is safe to do so.

(2) For the purpose of subsection (1), the Minister may, after
consultation with the Minister charged with the responsibility
for works, make rules relating to the movement of motor
vehicles and pedestrians and the erection of traffic signs at
such authorized left turn traffic junctions.

Minister charged with the responsibility for works in relation
to a Federal road and designated Federal Territory road, and
in any case after consultation with the Chief Police Officer,

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Road Transport Act 1987 (“Act 333”).

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and the power of the Minister to appoint_
the commencement date of the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 333 to introduce a new_
definition of “electric bicycle” and to amend certain existing definitions used in
Act 333.


-----

8 _Bill_

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection 5(1) of Act 333 to increase the unladen_
weight of invalid carriages, motor cars heavy and motor cars respectively.

5. _Clauses 4 and_ _5 seek to amend subsections 14(4) and 15(4) of Act 333_
respectively to provide for a minimum fine of three hundred ringgit for offences
under those sections.

6. _Clause 6_ seeks to amend subsection 17(1) of Act 333 to empower
a Director to refuse to grant a motor vehicle licence in relation to a class
or category of motor vehicles required to undergo periodic inspection under
Act 333 if the inspection certificate issued under section 66C of Act 333 is
not produced.

7. _Clause 7_ seeks to amend subsection 26(2) of Act 333 to provide for
a minimum fine of three hundred ringgit and to increase the maximum fine
to two thousand ringgit for offences under that section.

8. _Clauses 8_ and _9 seek_ to amend subsections 35(8) and 35A(6) of Act 333
respectively to provide that a person who fails to settle the penalty prescribed
for the relevant offence within three months from the issuance of the notice
under section 53, 53A, 115A or 119, as the case may be, shall be deemed
to have committed the said offence unless criminal proceedings have been
instituted for that offence.

9. _Clause 10 seeks to amend subsection 40(1) of Act 333 to provide for_
a minimum fine of three hundred ringgit and to increase the maximum fine
to two thousand ringgit for offences under that section.

10. _Clause 11 seeks to amend section 53A of Act 333 to allow notices under_
that section to be served as soon as reasonably practicable and to clarify the
contents of such notices.

11. _Clause 12 seeks to amend section 54 of Act 333 to extend the provisions_
relating to certain driving offences to electric bicycles.

12. _Clause 13_ seeks to amend section 56 of Act 333 to clarify the validity
period of a vocational licence. This clause also seeks to empower the Director
to suspend and revoke a vocational licence if the driving licence issued under
section 27 of Act 333 is suspended or revoked under section 32, 35 or 36 of
Act 333.

13. _Clauses 14 and 15 seek to amend subsections 59(9) and 66A(2) of Act 333_
respectively to provide for a minimum fine of three hundred ringgit for
offences under those sections.

14. _Clause 16 seeks to introduce a new section 76A into Act 333 to provide_
for authorized left turns.

15. _Clause 17 seeks to amend subsections 79(1) and 79(2) of Act 333_
respectively to empower a road transport officer to regulate traffic other than
a police officer and traffic warden. This _clause also seeks to provide for a_
minimum fine of three hundred ringgit and to increase the maximum fine to
two thousand ringgit for offences under those subsections.

16. _Clause 18 seeks to amend paragraphs 88(1)(d)_ and _(p) of Act 333 to_

17. _Clause 19 seeks to amend subsection 112(3) of Act 333 to empower_
