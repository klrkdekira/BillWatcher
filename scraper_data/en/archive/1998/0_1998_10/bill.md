EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Rubber Industry (Replanting) Fund
Ordinance 1952 ("the Ordinance").

2. Clause 1 contains the short title and provisions on the
commencement of the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 4 seeks to amend section 16 of the Ordinance to empower_

the Minister after consultation with the Minister of Finance to impose
and collect a cess on rubber for replanting purposes.

4. _Clause 6 seeks to introduce new Parts V and VI into the_

Ordinance.

Part V contains provisions relating to enforcement, investigation,

evidence and offences.

Part VI deals with general matters such as the compounding of

offences, institution of prosecution under the Act, jurisdiction to
try offences under the Act and power to exempt.

5. Amendments which are not specifically dealt with in this

Statement are minor or consequential in nature.
