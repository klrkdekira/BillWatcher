EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Printing of Qur'anic Texts Act 1986
(the "Act").

2. _Clause 1 deals with the short title and commencement of the_

proposed Act.


-----

_Bill_

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of the Act to improve on_

the definitions of "document" and "Qur'anic text".

4. Clause 3 seeks to amend section 3 of the Act to substitute for
the words "Islamic Religious Affairs Division" the words "Department
of Islamic Development Malaysia" and to reduce the quorum of
meeting of the Lembaga Pengawalan dan Pelesenan Pencetakan Al-
Qur'an (the "Lembaga") from nine to seven members.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend subsection 6(1) of the Act to require_

either the final proof or the original copy of the Qur'anic text to
be submitted to the Lembaga for certification before it is printed
or published.

6. Clause 5 seeks to amend subsection 8(1) of the Act to require
certain information to be provided on the printed first or last leaf
of the Qur'anic text or, in the case of a recorded text, at a conspicuous
part of the text.

7. Clause 6 seeks to amend subsection 12(3) of the Act to require
the certification of the Lembaga be made on the printed first or last
leaf of the Qur'anic text or, in the case of a recorded text, at a
conspicuous part of the text.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to amend subsection 13(1) of the Act to require_

the information specified in subsection 8(1) or the certificate specified
in subsection 12(3) of the Act to be shown on the printed first or
last leaf of the Qur'anic text or, in the case of a recorded text, at
a conspicuous part of the text.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

**This Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial**
expenditure. [PN. (U[2]) 2016.]
