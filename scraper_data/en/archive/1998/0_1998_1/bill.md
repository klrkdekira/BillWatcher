EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to repeal the Teachers' Superannuation Ordinance
1963 (Sabah) ("the Ordinance"), to dissolve the Teachers'
Superannuation Fund and the Board of Management established
under the Ordinance.

2. _Clauses 3 and 4 provide for the dissolution of the Board and the_
Fund and for the devolution of powers, rights, privileges, duties,
liabilities and obligations of the Board to the Government.

3. _Clause 5 provides for the transfer of all immovable property and_
other assets of the Fund to the Government.

4. _Clause 6 provides for the transfer of all moneys standing in, or_
due to be paid, to the Fund to the Government.

5. _Clause 7 provides for the enforcement of all existing contracts_
by or against the Government in substitution of the Board.

6. _Clause 8 provides that The State Treasurer of Sabah shall be_
responsible for the payment of pensions to the eligible persons.

7. _Clause 9 provides for the payment of pension in a lump sum_
amount to a person who was, on 15 February 1983, an eligible
person and the lump sum amount shall be paid out of the moneys
referred to in section 6.

8. _Clause 10 provides that any payment may be made under the_
proposed Act to the dependants of a deceased eligible person without
probate or letters of administration.

9. _Clause I I provides that any payment which is made in good_
faith shall constitute a full discharge of all the liabilities of the
Government under the Ordinance.

10. _Clause 12 provides for the continuance of legal proceedings_
by or against the Government in substitution of the Board.

11. Clause 13 provides for the conferment of powers to the Minister
to make orders for the purpose of removing any difficulties that may
arise in connection with the implementation of the proposed Act.
