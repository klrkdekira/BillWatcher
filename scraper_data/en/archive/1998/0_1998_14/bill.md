EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

The proposed Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission
Act 1998 ("the Act") seeks to establish a body corporate by the name
of "Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission" ("the
Commission") having power to supervise and regulate the
communications and multimedia activities in Malaysia including to
enforce certain legislations relating to communications and multimedia.
