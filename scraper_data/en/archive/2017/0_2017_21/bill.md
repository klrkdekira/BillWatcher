**ENACTED** by the Parliament of Malaysia as follows:

**Short title and commencement**

**1.** (1) This Act may be cited as the Lembaga Kemajuan Kelantan
Selatan (Amendment) Act 2017.

(2) This Act comes into operation on a date to be appointed
by the Minister by notification in the _Gazette._

**Amendment of section 2**

**2.** The Lembaga Kemajuan Kelantan Selatan Act 1978 [Act 203],
which is referred to as the “principal Act” in this Act, is amended
in section 2 by substituting for the interpretation of “Minister”
the following interpretation:

‘ “Minister” means the Minister charged with the responsibility
for rural and regional development;’.

**15. (1) Notwithstanding the provisions of this Act, the members**
of the Lembaga and the members of any committee appointed
or elected before the date of coming into operation of this Act
shall continue to hold office until the appointments are revoked
or until expiry of their terms of appointment.

(2) Any disciplinary proceedings instituted under sections 16a
to 16h of the principal Act which is pending immediately before
the date of coming into operation of this Act shall, after the
date of coming into operation of this Act, continue and be dealt
with as if sections 16a to 16h of the principal Act had not been
amended by this Act.

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Lembaga Kemajuan Kelantan Selatan Act 1978
(“Act 203”). The proposed amendment seeks to facilitate and expedite the
administration of the Lembaga and improve service delivery. The proposed
amendment is also in line with the normal practice of the Lembaga.

2. _Clause 1 contains the short title and the provision on the commencement_
of the proposed Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 to introduce the interpretation of_
“Minister” to make it clear that the Minister responsible for carrying out
the functions under Act 203 is the Minister in charge of rural and regional
development.

_Lembaga Kemajuan Kelantan Selatan (Amendment)_ 9

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend subsection 5(1) of Act 203 in respect of the_
appointment of the members of the Lembaga by the Minister. This clause also
seeks to delete subsection 5(4) which is no longer applicable to the General
Manager.

_Clause 3 also seeks to amend subsections 5(2) and (3) as a consequence of_

the amendment made to subsection 5(4) of Act 203 and to amend subsection 5(5)
as a consequence of the amendment made to subsection 5(1) of Act 203.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to introduce new section 5a into Act 203 to empower the_
Minister to appoint alternate members in relation to certain members of the
Lembaga.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend the shoulder note to section 12 of Act 203 to_
state the functions of the Chairman in general. Clause 5 also seeks to substitute
subsection 12(1) of Act 203 to provide the responsibility of the Chairman for
supervision and control over the implementation of policies and decisions
made by the Lembaga. This _clause also seeks to substitute subsection 12(2)_
of Act 203 to provide for the delegation of powers by the Chairman to any
other member, any officer or servant of the Lembaga.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend subsections 13(1) and (2) of Act 203 to differentiate_
the functions and responsibilities of the Chairman and the General Manager.
This _clause also seeks to amend subsections 13(3) of Act 203 to provide that_
the General Manager shall be responsible to the Minister and the Lembaga and
no longer to the Chairman. This Clause also seeks to amend subsection 13(4)
of Act 203 to provide that the General Manager shall have the administrative
control over the officers and servants of the Lembaga and shall not be subject
to the directions of the Chairman.

_Clause 6 also seek to amend subsection 13(5) of Act 203 to state that the_

General Manager shall perform such other or further duties as the Minister
or the Lembaga may determine and to delete the requirement for the General
Manager to perform such other or further duties as the Chairman from time
to time direct. The proposed new subsection 13(7a) into Act 203 seeks to
provide the delegation of powers of the General Manager to any officers or
servants of the Lembaga.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to amend subsection 14(2) of Act 203 to provide that_
the Manager of each Division shall be appointed by the Lembaga with the
approval of the Minister.

9. _Clause 8_ seeks to substitute section 16a of Act 203 to provide that the
Statutory Bodies (Discipline and Surcharge) Act 2000 [Act 605] shall apply
in respect of the disciplinary matters of the employees of the Lembaga.

10. _Clause 9_ seeks to delete sections 16b, 16c, 16d, 16e, 16f, 16g and

16h of Act 203 as a consequence of the amendment made to section 16a of
Act 203.

11. _Clause 10_ seeks to substitute section 22 of Act 203 to provide that the

Statutory Bodies (Accounts and Annual Reports) Act 1980 [Act 240] shall
apply to the Lembaga.

12. _Clause 11 seeks to introduce new section 28a into Act 203 to empower_

the Lembaga to establish committees to assist the Lembaga in performing its
functions.

13. _Clause 12_ seeks to introduce new section 32a into Act 203 to empower

the Lembaga, with the approval of the Minister, to amend the Schedules to
Act 203.

14. _Clause 13 seeks to amend subparagraphs 4(1) and 6(3) of the_

First Schedule to Act 203. The amendment to subparagraph 4(1) seeks to
reduce the number of meetings of the Lembaga from once a month to once
in every three months. The amendment to subparagraph 6(3) seeks to provide
that the common seal and stamp of the Lembaga shall be kept in the custody
of the General Manager.

_Clause 13 also seeks to introduce new subparagraph 4(2a) to state the_

voting right of every member of the Lembaga present.

15. _Clause 14 seeks to introduce new Third Schedule into Act 203 to_

provide the matters relating to the appointment, meetings and procedures of
the committee established under Act 203.

16. _Clause 15 seeks to provide saving and transitional provisions._

17. Other amendments not specifically dealt with in this Explanatory Statement

are minor or consequential in nature.

_FINANCIAL IMPLICATION_

The Bill will not involve the Government in any extra financial expenditure.
