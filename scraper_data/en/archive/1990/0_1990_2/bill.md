SERI PAHLAWAN GAGAH PERKASA 7
(REMEMBRANCE ALLOWANCE)

3. Clause 3 seeks to provide for the grant of a remembrance
allowance of two hundred ringgit per month to a recipient of the
Decoration of Seri Pahlawan Gagah Perkasa. The remembrance
allowance shall be paid retrospectively commencing with the
month in which the Decoration is conferred on the recipient, but
not earlier than June 1983.

4. Clause 4 seeks to provide for the cessation of a grant of
remembrance allowance and its restoration on the occurrences
stipulated therein.

5. Clause 5 seeks to provide for the grant of a total sum of twenty
thousand ringgit once only to the next-of-kin of a recipient where
the Decoration of Seri Pahlawan Gagah Perkasa is conferred
posthumously.

6. Clause 6 seeks to provide for the grant of a total sum of twenty
thousand ringgit once only to the next-of-kin of a recipient where
the Decoration of Seri Pahlawan Gagah Perkasa was conferred
before the commencement of this Bill and the recipient had died
before such date.

7. Clause 7 empowers the Prime Minister to apportion the
amounts to be paid to a deceased recipient's next-of-kin.

8. Clause 8 provides that the remembrance allowances and sums
granted under this Bill shall be charged on the Federal
Consolidated Fund, while clause 9 provides that such
remembrance allowances and sums shall not be assignable,
transferable, etc.

_FINANCIAL_ _IMPLICATIONS_

This Bill will involve the Government in extra financial
expenditure the amount of which cannot at present
be ascertained. [PN. (IP) 1336.]
