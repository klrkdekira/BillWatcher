EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Securities Industry Act 1983,
hereinafter referred to as the "Act", and references in this Statement
to a "section", "subsection" or "paragraph" is a reference to a
section, subsection or paragraph, respectively, in that Act.

2. _Clause 1 deals with the commencement date of the Bill._

3. Clause 2 seeks to amend subsection 2(1) which contains
definitions of words and expressions used in the Act. The deletion
of words and expressions and the introduction of various new words
and expression are consequential to the establishment of the
Securities Commission under the Securities Commission Act 1992.


-----

12 BILL

4. _Clause 3 seeks to increase the penalty for the offence under_
section 7.

5. Clause 4 seeks to amend section 8 to empower the Minister
to appoint the Executive Chairman of the committee of the stock
exchange.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend section 9 to transfer the power of the_
Minister under that section to the Commission.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to amend section 10 to confer upon the_
Licensing Officer and the Investigating Officer of the Commission
the powers to perform their functions and duties. This clause also
seeks to increase the penalty for the offence under section 10.

8. Clause 7 seeks to amend section 11. The proposed amendment
seeks to empower the stock exchange to take action against any
person for failure to comply with the rules or the listing
requirements.

9. Clause 8 seeks to introduce a new section 15A which empowers
the Minister to appoint a Licensing Officer who will be responsible
for matters relating to licensing under the Act. These matters are
currently the responsibility of the Registrar of Companies.

10. _Clause 9 seeks to amend sections 16, 17, 18. 19, 20 and 21._
The amendments are consequential to the introduction of the new
section 15A.

11. _Clause 10 seeks to increase the penalty for the offence under_
section 22.

12. Clause 11 seeks to amend sections 23, 25, 26, 27, 28 and 31.
The amendments are consequential to the introduction of the new
section 15A.

13. _Clauses 12, 13 and J4 seek to amend sections 33, 34 and 35_
to give the Commission certain powers under the Act.

14. _Clause 15 seeks to amend section 37. The amendment is_
consequential to the introduction of the new section 15A.

15. Clauses 16. 17 and 18 seek to amend sections 39, 40 and 41.
The amendments seek to increase the penalties under those sections.

16. _Clause 19 seeks to amend section 43 to replace the word_
"Registrar" with the word "Commission".

17. Clause 20 seeks to introduce a new subsection ( 1 A ) into
section 44. This subsection provides that any cheque issued but not


-----

collected within five bank trading days after the date of such issue
shall be credited into the trust account. It also seeks to increase the
penalty for an offence committed under that section.

18. _Clauses 21, 22, 23. 24, 25, 26 and 27 seek to amend sections_

49, 51, 52, 53, 54, 55, 56 and 57. The amendments are
consequential to the establishment of the Commission whereby the
powers of the Minister under the various sections are transferred
to the Commission. The amendments are also consequential to the
introduction of section 15A.

19. _Clause 28 seeks to increase the penalty for an offence under_

section 58.

20. _Clauses 29, 30 and 31 seek to amend sections 68, 69 and 72._

The amendments are consequential to the establishment of the

Commission whereby the powers of the Minister and Registrar
under those sections are transferred to the Commission.

21. _Clauses 32, 33, 34, 35, 36 and 37 seek to increase the penalty_

for offences under sections 91, 92, 96, 97. 98 and 100.

22. _Clause 38 seeks to delete the whole of Division 2 of Part X._

With the establishment of the Commission, the various powers of
the Minister under that Division are no longer necessary.

23. _Clause 39 seeks to increase the penalty for an offence under_

section 123.

24. _Clause 40 seeks to introduce a new section 124 to empower_

the Licensing Officer to compound certain offences against the Act.

25. _Clause 41 seeks to introduce a new section 126. relating to_

the conduct of prosecution.

26. _Clause 42 contains savings provisions relating to the_

amendment of Part IV of the Act.
