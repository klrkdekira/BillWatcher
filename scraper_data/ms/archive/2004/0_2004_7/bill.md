# 14                                  Bill

EXPLANATORY STATEMENT

This Bill seeks to amend the Pesticides Act 1974 ("Act 149").

_2._ _Clause 1 contains the short title and the provisions on the date of_
commencement of the proposed amendment Act.

3. _Clause 2 seeks to amend section 2 of Act 149 to introduce new definitions_

and to amend definitions of certain terms used in the Act.

4. _Clause 3 seeks to amend section 3 of Act 149 by increasing the membership_
of the Board to fourteen members, and amending the designation of the posts
held by the existing members of the Board to conform with the current designation
for such posts.

5. _Clause 4 seeks to amend subsection 6(4) of the Act by increasing the_

quorum required for a meeting of the Board to seven members.

6. _Clause 5 seeks to amend section 7 of the Act to require a person who_

intends to register a pesticide to submit to the Board a sample of the pesticide,
in addition to the requirements presently provided for under that section. This
_clause also requires an application to register a pesticide to be accompanied_
with the prescribed application fee.

7. _Clause 6 seeks to introduce a new subsection (3A) into section 8 of Act_

149
to enable the Board to issue guidelines regarding the classes of pesticides under
which pesticides are to be classified.

8. _Clause 7 seeks to amend section 9 of Act 149 to increase the period of_

registration of a pesticide to five years.

9. _Clause 8 seeks to amend section 10 of Act 149 to empower the Board to_

cancel the registration of a pesticide if the Board considers that the pesticide
has adverse effects on animals, plants, fruits or property.
